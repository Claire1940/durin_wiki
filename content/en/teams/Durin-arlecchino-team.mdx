<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mastering the Rotation: Combat Strategies">

The effectiveness of a **Durin arlecchino team** hinges on the precision of your rotation. Because Arlecchino cannot be healed through traditional means while in combat, the timing of Durin's defensive utility and resistance shred is critical for survival and damage output.

**Standard Overload Rotation:**

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Marking">
    Start with Arlecchino's Elemental Skill to apply the Blood-Debt Directive to all nearby enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Support Setup">
    Switch to Chevreuse and use her Skill/Burst to trigger the first Overload and activate her 40% RES shred passive.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Durin Activation">
    Use Durin's Elemental Skill twice (E+E) to enter the **White Flame** form, followed immediately by his Elemental Burst to summon the Dragon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Electro Application">
    Deploy Fischl's Oz to ensure a constant source of Electro for continuous Overload reactions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Arlecchino DPS">
    Switch back to Arlecchino, perform a Charged Attack to absorb the marks, and unleash her Normal Attack string.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Rotation Warning">
Failing to trigger an Overload reaction with Chevreuse *before* Durin's Burst will result in a significant loss of total team damage. Always ensure the "Overloaded" text appears before committing to Durin's animations.
</Callout>

**Vaporize/Melt Strategy:**
For players focusing on single-target damage, the rotation shifts slightly to prioritize Durin's **Dark Decay** form. This form specifically enhances reaction damage, making it the superior choice when paired with Hydro or Cryo applicators like Yelan or Rosaria.

</Section>
