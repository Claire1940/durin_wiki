<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Mechanics and Performance Comparison">

To understand whether to pull for **durin or mavuika**, one must look at their elemental application frequency and internal cooldowns (ICD). Mavuika provides Pyro application through her skill, which is convenient for overworld exploration and long-duration fights. Durin's application is tied to his Burst, which hits significantly harder and provides additional utility that Mavuika lacks at C0.

| Feature | Durin (Off-Field) | Mavuika (Archon) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Source of Pyro** | Elemental Burst | Elemental Skill |
| **Application Speed** | High (Multi-hit) | Moderate (Standard) |
| **Buffing Utility** | 30% Vape Buff, Shred | Nightsoul, Atk Buff |
| **Weapon Type** | Sword/Catalyst Hybrid | Claymore |
| **Primary Artifact** | Cinder City / New Set | Obsidian Codex |
| **Team Role** | Support / Sub-DPS | Main DPS / Driver |

Durin’s kit is specifically designed to enhance elemental reactions. His unique passive provides a 30% damage increase to Vaporize reactions, making him the premier partner for Hydro carries. Mavuika, while capable of buffing the team, focuses more on her own damage scaling through the Nightsoul system prevalent in Natlan characters.

<Callout type="warning" title="Energy Requirements">
Durin is highly dependent on his Elemental Burst. Without at least 180% Energy Recharge, his off-field Pyro application will suffer from significant downtime, unlike Mavuika who can maintain Pyro via her Skill.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Buffing Capabilities and Hexerei Synergy">

The "Hexerei" mechanic has become a cornerstone of the 6.x meta. Durin is built to maximize this system, particularly when paired with older characters who have received Hexerei updates. In testing, Durin’s ability to shred elemental resistances (up to 30% at specific constellations) allows him to act as a secondary buffer that rivals even Bennett in specific setups.

| Buff Type | Durin's Contribution | Mavuika's Contribution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Elemental Shred** | 20-30% (Constellation dependent) | Minimal (Requires specific gear) |
| **Reaction Bonus** | 30% Vaporize / Melt DMG | Standard Reaction scaling |
| **Nightsoul Support** | Moderate generation | Massive generation |
| **Atk Scaling** | Team-wide via Signature Weapon | Self-scaling / Archon Passive |

In teams involving Mona or Venti, Durin activates the Hexerei bonus more efficiently than Mavuika. This is because Durin's kit is "reaction-hungry," meaning he benefits more from the rapid elemental application provided by Hexerei supports. Mavuika is often "too strong" for these teams, sometimes stealing reactions from the main carry due to her high poise damage and heavy hits.

<Callout type="success" title="Strategy Tip">
When using Durin, always initiate your rotation with his Burst to ensure the 30% Vape buff is active before switching to your Hydro or Cryo main DPS. This maximizes the window of high-damage output.
</Callout>
