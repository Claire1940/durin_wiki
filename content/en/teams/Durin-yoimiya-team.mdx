### Team Rotation Steps
A proper rotation ensures all buffs are active and elemental applications are consistent for maximum damage in your **Durin Yoimiya team**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start with Durin">
    Use Durin's Skill twice to enter his "Confirmation of Purity" state, then use his Elemental Burst. This provides off-field Pyro application and RES Shred.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Activate Chevreuse">
    Switch to Chevreuse. Hold her Skill to generate particles and apply Electro/Pyro RES Shred, then use her Burst for additional buffs and healing. (If C6, this also provides a significant ATK% boost).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Deploy Fischl">
    Switch to Fischl and use her Elemental Skill (Oz) or Burst (to refresh Oz) for consistent off-field Electro application. This is crucial for triggering Overload with Durin's Pyro.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Yoimiya On-Field DPS">
    Swap to Yoimiya. Use her Elemental Skill, then perform her normal attack string to trigger numerous Overload reactions. Optionally, use her Burst early in the rotation to buff teammates' attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Repeat">
    Once Yoimiya's Skill duration ends, repeat the rotation starting with Durin. Manage energy for all characters to ensure Bursts are ready for each cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>
