The synergy between **Durin** and **Kinich** represents a significant shift in the Dendro-Pyro meta as of May 2026. While Durin is traditionally incentivized to run within **Hexerei** teams to unlock his full potential, his performance as a solo-Hexerei unit in a **Durin Kinich team** remains elite. Durin's **Principle of Purity** (Light Form) is the engine of this team, offering the off-field presence required to keep the fire alive while Kinich dominates the field.

<Section icon={Target} title="Combat Rotations & Skill Management">

Executing the correct rotation is vital to ensure that Kinich enters the field while all buffs are active and the enemy is already under the **Burning** status. In a **Durin Kinich team**, Durin should always act immediately before Kinich to maximize the uptime of his RES shred and the **Blessing of Pastoral Winds** effect.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Application">
    Start with **Emilie's** Elemental Skill to establish a Dendro aura and set up the Lumidouce Case for off-field damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Buff Setup">
    Switch to **Bennett** and cast his Elemental Burst to create the ATK-buffing field. Use his Skill if extra energy is needed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Durin's Convergence">
    Switch to **Durin**. Use his Elemental Skill, then a **Normal Attack** to enter **Confirmation of Purity** (Light Form). Immediately cast his **Elemental Burst**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Kinich Domination">
    Switch to **Kinich**. Use his Skill to grapple and begin his high-damage loop. The Burning reaction triggered by Durin will keep Kinich's passives active throughout the duration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Rotation Warning">
Do NOT use Durin's **Dark Form** (Denial of Darkness) in this team. Dark Form focuses on personal Vaporize/Melt damage and will significantly lower the team's overall synergy with Kinich's Dendro application.
</Callout>

</Section>
