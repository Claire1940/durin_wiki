<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Durin's Core Mechanics">
Durin, a 5-star Pyro Sword character, offers incredible versatility in team compositions thanks to his unique dual-mode playstyle. He can function as an off-field Pyro Sub-DPS/Support in his White Mode or an on-field Pyro DPS in his Dark Mode. Understanding how these modes affect your **Durin team comp** is crucial for maximizing his potential.

**Video Highlights:**
- Durin's dual modes: White (Purity) and Dark (Darkness).
- How to switch between modes using Elemental Skill and Normal Attack.
- White Mode: Off-field AoE Pyro, RES shred for Overload, Burning, Pyro Swirl, Crystallize.
- Dark Mode: Single-target Pyro, increased personal Vaporize/Melt damage.
- Hexerei Resonance: Significant buffs when paired with another Hexerei character.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="xHJHKPpwBes" title="COMPLETE DURIN GUIDE! Best Durin Build [ALL Playstyles] - Artifacts, Weapons, Teams | Genshin Impact" />

Durin's Elemental Skill allows him to enter one of two forms. In **White Mode**, his Elemental Burst provides continuous off-field AoE Pyro application and shreds elemental resistances (Pyro, Electro, Dendro, Geo, Anemo) by 20% (or 35% with Hexerei Resonance) when triggering reactions other than Vaporize or Melt. This makes him an excellent support for a wide array of team compositions. In **Dark Mode**, his Elemental Burst deals higher single-target Pyro damage and gains a significant damage bonus to Vaporize and Melt reactions (40% base, 70% with Hexerei Resonance), positioning him as a strong on-field carry.

<Callout type="tip" title="Hexerei Resonance Advantage">
Pairing Durin with another Hexerei character (e.g., Fischl, Mona, Albedo) is highly recommended. This activates the Hexerei Resonance, boosting his resistance shred in White Mode and his personal Vaporize/Melt damage in Dark Mode by an additional 75% of their base values, making his team contributions even more impactful.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Optimizing Your Durin Team Comp Strategy">
Building a successful **Durin team comp** isn't just about character selection; it also involves understanding rotation, energy management, and artifact choices.

### Rotation Fundamentals

Proper rotation ensures maximum uptime on buffs and consistent elemental reactions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Buffs">
    Start with your team's buffers (e.g., Bennett's Burst, Chevreuse's Skill) to apply buffs and generate particles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Durin's Skill & Mode Selection">
    Swap to Durin, use his Elemental Skill (twice for White Mode, Skill + Normal Attack for Dark Mode). This generates energy and sets his form.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Durin's Burst">
    Immediately use Durin's Elemental Burst to apply off-field Pyro and activate his mode-specific buffs/damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Main DPS Field Time">
    Swap to your main DPS to benefit from all active buffs and Durin's off-field abilities. Execute their primary damage rotation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Recharge and Repeat">
    After the DPS rotation, use other characters' skills to generate energy and prepare for the next rotation. Durin's skill refund helps a lot here.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Energy Recharge Requirements

Durin's energy refund from his Elemental Skill significantly reduces his ER needs.

| Team Type                | ER Requirement (Approx.) | Notes                                                     |
| :----------------------- | :----------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Double Pyro/Overload** | 110-120%                 | Very low due to Pyro resonance and Chevreuse/Bennett.     |
| **Solo Pyro (Off-field)**| 130-150%                 | More needed if he's the sole Pyro unit generating particles. |
| **Burgeon (Full EM)**    | 140-160%                 | Higher ER ensures burst uptime for consistent Burgeon.    |

<Callout type="info" title="Artifact Sets for Durin">
The new artifact set, **A Day Carved from Rising Winds**, is generally Durin's Best-in-Slot for both his Sub-DPS and On-field roles, especially with Hexerei members. Otherwise, **Noblesse Oblige** (Sub-DPS) or **Crimson Witch of Flames** (On-field DPS) are strong alternatives. For Burgeon, **Gilded Dreams** or **Flower of Paradise Lost** are ideal.
</Callout>
