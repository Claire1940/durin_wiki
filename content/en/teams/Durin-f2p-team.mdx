<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Durin's F2P Potential">
Durin, a 5-star Pyro Sword character, excels as a Hexerei Pyro Sub-DPS. While his full potential is often unlocked with premium weapons and specific Hexerei teammates, it is entirely possible to build a highly effective **Durin F2P team** that can clear challenging content like the Spiral Abyss and Stygian Onslaught. The key lies in understanding his kit and pairing him with strong 4-star supports who can enable his damage.

Durin's kit revolves around his Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst, which can take on "Light" or "Dark" forms. The Light Form provides consistent Pyro application and RES shredding, making him an excellent off-fielder for Burning, Double-Geo, Swirl, and Overloaded teams. The Dark Form focuses on personal damage, specializing in Vaporize or Melt reactions. For F2P teams, leveraging his off-field Pyro application and reaction potential is crucial.

<Section icon={Map} title="Team Rotation and Strategy">
Effective team rotation is key to maximizing damage in any **Durin F2P team**. Here's a general approach:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Apply Elemental Aura">
    Start by applying the necessary elemental aura on enemies. For Vaporize, use Xingqiu's Elemental Skill and Burst. For Overload, use Fischl's Skill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Support Buffs and Shred">
    Deploy Bennett's Elemental Burst for ATK buff and healing. If using Sucrose, use her Skill/Burst to Swirl the element and apply Viridescent Venerer's RES shred.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Durin's Elemental Burst">
    Switch to Durin and use his Elemental Burst. If in Dark Form for Vaporize/Melt, ensure the Hydro/Cryo aura is active. If in Light Form, his off-field Pyro application will trigger reactions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Follow-up and Recharge">
    After Durin's Burst, continue with your Sub-DPS or Anemo character to maintain elemental application and generate energy for the next rotation. Repeat the cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>
