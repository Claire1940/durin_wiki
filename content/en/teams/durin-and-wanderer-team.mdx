<Section icon={Target} title="Optimized Rotation and Tactics">

Executing the correct rotation is what separates a good **durin and wanderer team** from a great one. You must ensure that Durin enters his "White Form" (Principle of Purity) before Wanderer takes the field. This ensures that the Pyro aura is active for Wanderer's Skill activation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Buffing">
    Start with Faruzan's Burst to shred Anemo RES. If using Bennett, cast his Burst immediately after to set the ATK field.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Durin's Setup">
    Use Durin's Elemental Skill, then follow up with his Elemental Burst. Ensure you are in the **Confirmation of Purity** state to trigger the RES shred and off-field Pyro strikes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Wanderer's Flight">
    Switch to Wanderer. Activate his Elemental Skill while standing inside Bennett's circle and near Durin's Pyro aura. Confirm the Pyro absorption icon is visible.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Damage Window">
    Spam Wanderer's Normal and Charged attacks. Use his Burst right before the flight meter expires to catch all active buffs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Pro Tip">
If Durin is C2, the team gains an additional 50% Pyro DMG bonus. This significantly boosts the "Swirl" damage and any additional Pyro reactions triggered during Wanderer's field time.
</Callout>
