<Section icon={Sprout} title="Advanced Tips and Team Optimization">
To truly master the Durin Neuvillette Burgeon team, consider these advanced tips for further optimization.

### Managing Dendro Core Generation

*   **Hydro-Dendro Ratio:** Aim for a slight excess of Hydro application to ensure consistent Dendro Core generation. Too much Dendro can lead to Quicken/Aggravate reactions instead of Burgeon.
*   **Enemy Grouping:** Utilize Anemo characters like Kazuha or Sucrose to group enemies, allowing Durin's Burgeon explosions to hit multiple targets for maximum efficiency.
*   **EM Stacking:** Ensure Durin has as much Elemental Mastery as possible through artifacts, weapon, and external buffs (e.g., Nahida's Burst, Sucrose's passives).

### Survivability Considerations

*   **Hydro Resonance:** Running two Hydro characters (Neuvillette + Furina/Kokomi) provides a 25% HP bonus, which is beneficial for Neuvillette's damage and overall team survivability.
*   **Shielding:** Characters like Zhongli can prevent interruption for Neuvillette's charged attacks and protect the team from Burgeon self-damage.
*   **Healing:** Constant healing from characters like Kokomi, YaoYao, or Baizhu can offset the self-damage from Burgeon, allowing for more aggressive play.

<Callout type="warning" title="Beware of Burning/Overload">
While Burgeon is the primary focus, ensure your Pyro application isn't so overwhelming that it leads to frequent Burning reactions (which deal continuous Pyro damage) or Overload (which can knock enemies away, making Burgeon harder to hit). A balanced application is key.
</Callout>
