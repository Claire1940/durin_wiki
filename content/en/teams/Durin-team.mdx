Durin is a revolutionary 5-star Pyro Sword character whose utility is split between two distinct states: **Confirmation of Purity** (White Form) and **Denial of Darkness** (Dark Form). <Section icon={Swords} title="Top-Tier Durin Team Compositions">

Building a **Durin team** requires balancing his form with the primary damage dealer's needs. Because Durin provides off-field Pyro application and unique RES shred, he excels in teams that traditional Pyro supports struggle to assist, such as Geo or Anemo-centric squads.

### 1. The Overload Core (Chevreuse Meta)
This is arguably Durin's most potent team. By combining Durin with **Fischl** and **Chevreuse**, you create a core that provides massive ATK buffs, Pyro/Electro RES shred, and consistent off-field damage. The fourth slot is flexible for any Electro or Pyro carry.

### 2. Geo Resonance & Shred
Durin's ability to shred Geo RES makes him an invaluable asset for **Navia** or **Itto**. He enables consistent Crystallize reactions while providing the rare resistance reduction that Geo teams usually lack outside of Zhongli.

### 3. Quickburn & Dendro Synergy
In teams with **Kinich** or **Emilie**, Durin provides the consistent Pyro needed to maintain the Burning aura. His ability to shred Dendro RES simultaneously makes him a superior choice over traditional Pyro options in these specific multi-element scenarios.

| Team Archetype | Main DPS | Key Supports | Durin Form |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Overload** | Raiden / Clorinde | Chevreuse, Fischl | White Form |
| **Vaporize** | Neuvillette / Mualani | Furina, Mona | Dark Form |
| **Burning** | Kinich | Emilie, Bennett | White Form |
| **Mono-Geo** | Itto / Navia | Albedo, Gorou | White Form |
| **Anemo Carry** | Venti / Wanderer | Faruzan, Bennett | White Form |

<Section icon={Target} title="Building Your Durin: Gear & Stats">

To maximize a **Durin team**, you must gear him appropriately for his role. While his damage is significant, his utility often takes priority. His A4 passive scales his Burst damage based on his ATK, capping at **2500 ATK** for a 75% damage multiplier.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Balance Energy Recharge">
    Aim for 120-150% ER if playing off-field with one other Pyro unit. If using his Skill twice per rotation, you can drop ER entirely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reach the ATK Threshold">
    Prioritize ATK% substats until you hit 2500 ATK to maximize the A4 passive's 75% DMG multiplier on the first 10 hits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimize Crit Ratio">
    Since Durin ascends with Crit Damage, use a Crit Rate circlet to maintain a 1:2 ratio for consistent off-field DPS.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Slot | Main Stat | Priority Substats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sands** | ATK% or ER% | Crit Rate, Crit DMG, ATK%, EM |
| **Goblet** | Pyro DMG or ATK% | Crit Rate, Crit DMG, ATK%, ER |
| **Circlet** | Crit Rate or DMG | ATK%, Crit, ER, EM |

<Callout type="warning" title="Artifact Alert">
The 4pc **Noblesse Oblige** is generally his best-in-slot for team utility. However, in personal DPS setups (Dark Form), **Crimson Witch of Flames** or **Gilded Dreams** can yield higher individual numbers.
</Callout>
