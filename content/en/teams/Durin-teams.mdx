<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Durin's Core Mechanics for Team Building">
Durin, a 5-star Pyro Sword character, offers exceptional versatility in Genshin Impact team compositions. His unique ability to switch between White and Dark Modes fundamentally changes his role, allowing him to function as an off-field Pyro support/Sub-DPS or a reaction-focused on-field DPS. Understanding these modes is crucial for building effective **Durin teams**.

In White Mode, Durin provides consistent off-field AoE Pyro application and significant Elemental Resistance shred (especially for Pyro and the element involved in non-Vaporize/Melt reactions). This makes him an invaluable support. Dark Mode, on the other hand, boosts his personal Vaporize and Melt reaction damage, transforming him into a potent on-field damage dealer. The Hexerei resonance, when paired with another Hexerei character, amplifies these effects, increasing RES shred in White Mode and personal reaction damage in Dark Mode.

- Elemental Skill's role in mode switching and energy generation.
- White Mode's resistance shred for various Pyro reactions (Overload, Burning, Swirl, Crystallize).
- Dark Mode's increased Vaporize/Melt damage for personal output.
- The significant buffs gained from Hexerei resonance.

<Section icon={Map} title="Building Your Durin Team: A Step-by-Step Approach">
Constructing an optimal **Durin team** involves several considerations, from choosing his mode to selecting complementary characters and artifacts.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Define Durin's Role">
    Decide if you want Durin as an off-field support/Sub-DPS (White Mode) or a reaction-focused DPS (Dark Mode). This choice dictates artifact sets, main stats, and team members.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select a Core Reaction/Element">
    Based on Durin's role, choose the primary elemental reaction you want to trigger (e.g., Overload, Vaporize, Melt, Burgeon, Burning). This will guide your selection of other elements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Choose Complementary Characters">
    - **Main DPS**: A character who can benefit from Durin's Pyro application or buffs (e.g., Electro DPS for Overload, Hydro DPS for Vaporize).
    - **Support/Enabler**: Characters who provide the other elements needed for reactions or offer significant buffs/healing (e.g., Fischl, Chevreuse, Xingqiu, Bennett).
    - **Hexerei Partner**: Prioritize a Hexerei character for enhanced resonance effects, if applicable (e.g., Mona, Albedo, Fischl).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Artifacts and Weapons">
    - **White Mode (Support)**: Noblesse Oblige, "A Day Carved from Rising Winds" (new set), Energy Recharge/ATK/CRIT.
    - **Dark Mode (Reaction DPS)**: Crimson Witch, Gilded Dreams, "A Day Carved from Rising Winds", EM/Pyro DMG/CRIT.
    - **Weapons**: His signature weapon (Athame Artis) is often BiS. Freedom-Sworn for support, or crit/ATK stat sticks for DPS.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Refine Rotations and Energy Management">
    Ensure smooth rotations where Durin's Burst is consistently available. His Elemental Skill provides significant energy, often reducing his ER requirements. Adjust ER stats based on team particle generation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Artifact Set Considerations">
While "A Day Carved from Rising Winds" is generally strong, Noblesse Oblige remains a solid choice for White Mode Durin if another team member isn't already using it. For Burgeon teams, full Elemental Mastery is recommended.
</Callout>
