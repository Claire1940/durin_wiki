<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Durin's Core Mechanics for Team Building">
Durin, the newest Pyro five-star character in Genshin Impact, offers immense versatility as a Sub-DPS, support, and even a main damage dealer. His kit revolves around two distinct modes: White Mode and Dark Mode, each tailored for different team compositions and playstyles. Mastering these modes is crucial for building his best teams.

**Video Highlights:**
- Durin's Elemental Skill allows him to switch between White Mode and Dark Mode.
- White Mode provides continuous off-field Pyro application and resistance shred for various reactions.
- Dark Mode boosts Durin's personal Vaporize and Melt damage.
- Hexerei Resonance significantly enhances Durin's passive abilities in both modes.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="xHJHKPpwBes" title="COMPLETE DURIN GUIDE! Best Durin Build [ALL Playstyles] - Artifacts, Weapons, Teams | Genshin Impact" />

In White Mode, Durin's Elemental Burst offers consistent off-field AoE Pyro application and shreds resistances (Pyro and other elements involved in reactions like Overload, Burning, Pyro Swirl, Pyro Crystallize). This makes him an excellent support for increasing overall team damage. Conversely, Dark Mode focuses on his personal damage, granting a substantial bonus to his Vaporize and Melt reactions, turning him into a potent on-field or off-field reaction DPS. The Hexerei Resonance, activated by pairing Durin with another Hexerei character, further amplifies these effects, increasing RES shred to 35% in White Mode and personal reaction damage to 70% in Dark Mode.

<Callout type="tip" title="Hexerei Synergy">
Always consider a second Hexerei character for Durin's teams. This resonance provides significant buffs, enhancing his RES shred in White Mode or his personal Vaporize/Melt damage in Dark Mode, making many of his strongest teams feature this synergy.
</Callout>

### Burgeon Teams

Durin can be a potent Burgeon trigger, especially in AoE scenarios. For these teams, he should be in White Mode and built with Elemental Mastery to maximize the Burgeon reaction damage.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Hydro Application">
    Pair Durin with a fast Hydro applicator like **Neuvillette** or **Kokomi** to generate Dendro Cores.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Dendro Core Generation">
    Include a reliable Dendro character such as **Nahida** or **Lauma** to create Dendro Cores.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Durin's Role (White Mode EM)">
    Build Durin with full Elemental Mastery and activate his White Mode to trigger Burgeon. His AoE Pyro application is ideal for this.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Flex Slot for Damage/Utility">
    The last slot can be a flexible option like **Fischl** for Hexerei synergy and additional damage, or a shielder/healer if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Burgeon Build Note">
For optimal Burgeon damage, Durin should be built with **Elemental Mastery on all main artifact stats** (Sands, Goblet, Circlet). This is a specialized build distinct from his typical ATK/CRIT-focused builds.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Team Rotation and Playstyle Tips">
Executing Durin's rotations effectively ensures maximum uptime on his buffs and damage. His dual-mode nature means understanding when and how to activate each is key.

### Basic Rotation Principles

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose Mode">
    Decide whether to enter White Mode (Skill twice) or Dark Mode (Skill then Normal Attack) based on your team's needs before using his Burst.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Apply Buffs Early">
    As a support, use Durin's Burst (in the chosen mode) early in the rotation to apply Pyro and RES shred (White Mode) or set up for his personal reaction damage (Dark Mode) before your main DPS takes the field.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Energy">
    Durin's Elemental Skill refunds energy. Ensure you cast his skill at least once, or twice if in White Mode, to recover energy for his next Burst.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="On-Field DPS (Dark Mode)">
    If playing on-field Dark Mode Durin (especially with C6 Bennett), prioritize Normal Attack > Charged Attack combos to maximize Pyro application and trigger reactions. Jump or dash cancel Charged Attacks for faster combos.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Burst Snapshots">
Durin's Burst **does not snapshot** buffs. This means his damage will dynamically update with active buffs (e.g., from Bennett's field). Plan your rotations to ensure key buffs are active when his Burst hits.
</Callout>
