### Durin's Elemental Skill and Burst Synergy

Durin's Elemental Skill allows him to enter either the Confirmation of Purity (White) or Denial of Darkness (Dark) state. For a Vaporize-focused team with Yelan, the Denial of Darkness state is often preferred as it caters to Durin's personal damage, increasing his Vaporize and Melt damage. His Elemental Burst then functions differently based on the chosen state, with the Dark Form enhancing his reaction damage.

| Durin's Form | Primary Role | Key Benefits with Yelan |
| :----------- | :----------- | :---------------------- |
| **Light (Purity)** | Pyro Sub-DPS, RES Shred | Consistent Pyro application, minor RES shred, good for enabling Yelan's Hydro damage. |
| **Dark (Darkness)** | On-Field/Off-Field DPS | Increased personal Vaporize/Melt DMG, viable as an on-field driver for reactions. |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Team Rotations and Synergies">
Mastering the rotation is vital for maximizing the damage output of your **Durin Yelan team**. The goal is to ensure continuous Hydro application from Yelan while Durin triggers Vaporize with his Burst.

### Standard Vaporize Rotation

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate with Yelan">
    Use Yelan's Elemental Skill to generate energy, then immediately cast her Elemental Burst to apply off-field Hydro.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Durin's Setup">
    Switch to Durin. Use his Elemental Skill twice to enter the Denial of Darkness state (if aiming for personal Vaporize damage), then unleash his Elemental Burst.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Support Buffs">
    Activate buffs from your support characters (e.g., Bennett's Burst for ATK buff, Sucrose's Skill/Burst for Elemental Mastery and VV shred).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="On-Field DPS (if applicable)">
    If Durin is designed as an on-field DPS in his Dark Form, use his Normal Attacks during Yelan's Burst duration to trigger Vaporize. Otherwise, switch back to Yelan or a different sub-DPS.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Repeat">
    Repeat the rotation as cooldowns allow, ensuring Yelan's Burst is active to maintain Hydro application for Durin.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Overvape Team Composition with Yelan and Durin

For an Overvape team, you'll introduce an Electro character to trigger both Vaporize and Overload reactions. This adds another layer of reaction damage.

| Character | Role | Key Contribution |
| :-------- | :--- | :--------------- |
| **Durin** | Pyro Sub-DPS | Consistent Pyro application, Vaporize/Overload trigger. |
| **Yelan** | Hydro Sub-DPS/Buffer | Consistent off-field Hydro, DMG% buff. |
| **Fischl** | Electro Sub-DPS | Consistent off-field Electro application, high personal damage. |
| **Bennett** | Healer/Buffer | Massive ATK buff, healing, Pyro resonance. |

<Callout type="info" title="Rotation for Overvape (Durin, Yelan, Fischl, Bennett)">
1.  **Bennett:** Elemental Skill, then Elemental Burst.
2.  **Fischl:** Elemental Skill (Oz) or Elemental Burst.
3.  **Yelan:** Elemental Skill, then Elemental Burst.
4.  **Durin:** Elemental Skill (twice for Dark Form), then Elemental Burst.
5.  On-field with **Durin** or **Bennett** (if C6) to trigger reactions.
</Callout>

This setup provides a balanced approach to elemental reactions, leveraging Durin's Pyro, Yelan's Hydro, and Fischl's Electro for sustained damage.
