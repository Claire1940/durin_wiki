<Section icon={Target} title="Rotation and Strategy Guide">

Executing the correct rotation is vital for the **durin zibai team**. Because Zibai's damage relies on Radiance stacks and active buffs from her teammates, mistiming a switch can lead to a significant DPS loss.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Setup & Shielding">
    Start with your defensive support. If using Zhongli, hold his Skill to generate a shield. If using Xilonen, use her Skill to prepare the RES shred.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Hydro Application">
    Switch to Columbina or Aino. Deploy their Skill and Burst to ensure a constant Hydro aura on enemies. This is the foundation for Lunar-Crystallize.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="RES Shred & Buffing">
    Switch to Durin. Use his Skill twice followed by his Burst. This activates his RES shred and ensures the enemy is prepared for Zibai's entry.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Zibai's Burst Window">
    Switch to Zibai. Use her Burst immediately to gain Radiance, followed by her Skill to infuse Normal Attacks with Geo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Radiance Consumption">
    Spam Normal Attacks. Once Zibai has max Radiance stacks, use her Special Elemental Skill for a massive damage finisher before restarting the loop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Optimization Tip">
If you are running the Mono Geo variant with Albedo, place his flower (Skill) at the very beginning of the rotation. Its long duration allows it to persist through the entire setup.
</Callout>

</Section>
