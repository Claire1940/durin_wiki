<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimal Rotations and Gameplay Tips">
Executing efficient rotations is key to maximizing the damage output of your **Durin Overload teams**. The goal is to ensure continuous Pyro and Electro application, frequent Overload triggers, and optimal buff uptime.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Setup & Buffs">
    Start by deploying your primary support characters. For example, if using Chevreuse, use her Skill to apply Electro and trigger her buffs. If using Bennett, use his Burst for the ATK buff. Anemo characters like Sucrose or Kazuha should use their Skills/Bursts to swirl elements and shred resistances.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Durin's Elemental Skill & Burst">
    Swap to Durin. Use his Elemental Skill (ensuring you enter White Durin form by pressing the Skill button again) to apply Pyro and generate energy. Immediately follow with his Elemental Burst to activate his off-field Pyro application and resistance shred.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Electro DPS On-field/Skill">
    Bring in your Electro DPS (e.g., Fischl's Oz, Raiden's Skill/Burst). This character will trigger consistent Overload reactions with Durin's off-field Pyro. Focus on maximizing their damage during Durin's Burst window.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Repeat & Adapt">
    As cooldowns reset, repeat the rotation. Pay attention to energy levels and enemy types. If enemies are spread out, group them with Anemo utilities. Adjust your rotation based on whether you need more energy, healing, or burst damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Rotation Synergy">
The key to a powerful **Durin Overload team** is seamless elemental application. Durin's off-field Pyro, combined with rapid Electro application, creates a constant chain of Overload reactions. Ensure your buffs (like Chevreuse's or Bennett's) are active during the main damage windows.
</Callout>

### Advanced Tips for Durin Overload Teams

- **Energy Management:** Durin's Elemental Skill generates flat energy, reducing his ER requirements, especially if crowned. However, always monitor your team's energy needs to ensure everyone's Bursts are ready.
- **Hexerei Synergy:** If you have a second Hexerei character, ensure they are part of your rotation to maximize Durin's buffs.
- **Crowd Control:** Overload reactions naturally provide some stagger and knockback. Utilize Anemo characters for additional grouping to keep enemies within the AoE of Overload.
- **Situational Awareness:** While White Durin is generally better for Overload due to RES shred, Dark Durin can be considered for personal damage if the team composition allows for consistent Vaporize/Melt setups alongside Overload.

</Section>
