<Section icon={Trophy} title="Optimizing Durin's Rotation with Venti">
Effective rotation is vital for maximizing the damage output of a **Durin Venti team**. The goal is to ensure Venti's Burst captures Pyro from Durin for powerful Swirls, while Durin's RES shred is active for the main damage window.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate with Durin's Skill (White Form)">
    Use Durin's Elemental Skill to enter "Confirmation of Purity" mode. This applies off-field Pyro and triggers his flat energy generation, setting up RES shred for later.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Venti's Elemental Skill and Burst">
    Immediately follow with Venti's Elemental Skill to generate Anemo particles, then unleash his Elemental Burst to group enemies and absorb Durin's Pyro. This creates a powerful Pyro Swirl.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Support Buffs and Sub-DPS">
    Deploy support characters like Bennett (Burst for ATK buff) or Fischl (Oz for Electro application) to further buff the team and apply additional elements for reactions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Durin's Elemental Burst">
    Cast Durin's Elemental Burst to deal significant off-field Pyro damage, benefiting from Venti's Anemo RES shred and any active Hexerei buffs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="On-Field DPS (if applicable) or Repeat">
    If running an on-field DPS (e.g., Venti with his enhanced Normal Attacks or C6 Bennett Durin), switch to them for the damage window. Otherwise, repeat the rotation once cooldowns allow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Flexible Rotations">
Durin offers flexibility in his rotation. You can sometimes skip his skill before Burst if energy isn't an issue, or use his skill after Burst to funnel particles to other characters. Experiment to find what best suits your specific team and energy needs.
</Callout>

</Section>
