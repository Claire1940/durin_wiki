<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Mechanics and Application Comparison">

Understanding the technical differences between these two units is essential for optimizing Varesa's damage floor. The primary distinction lies in the source of their Pyro application and their internal cooldowns (ICD).

| Feature | Durin (Burst-Based) | Mavuika (Skill-Based) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Application Source** | Elemental Burst | Elemental Skill |
| **Uptime** | 12s / 15s Cooldown | 18s / 20s Cooldown |
| **Energy Needs** | High (80 Energy Cost) | Low (Skill-focused) |
| **AoE Range** | Large Circular Field | Targeted Orbital |
| **Reaction Frequency** | Fast (Standard ICD) | Moderate (Special ICD) |

Durin's Burst, "Midnight Fantasmical," creates a localized zone that deals continuous Pyro damage. This is particularly effective in Abyss scenarios where enemies are grouped. Mavuika, on the other hand, provides an orbital Pyro application that follows the active character, making her superior for mobile bosses or multi-wave encounters where enemies are spread apart.

<Callout type="warning" title="Energy Recharge Warning">
When using Durin, aim for at least 220% Energy Recharge if he is the solo Pyro unit. Failing to trigger his Burst on every rotation will result in a 40% DPS loss for Varesa.
</Callout>
