<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Durin's Vape Potential">
Durin, a 5-star Pyro Sword character, offers incredible flexibility in team compositions. While his White Form excels in off-field Pyro application and resistance shred, his Dark Form is specifically designed to amplify his personal damage, particularly through Vaporize and Melt reactions. Building an effective **Durin Vape Team** involves understanding his unique mechanics and pairing him with characters that enable consistent Hydro application.

**Video Highlights:**
- Durin's two forms: White (Purity) for support and Dark (Darkness) for personal damage.
- Dark Durin gains significant Vaporize and Melt damage bonuses.
- Hexerei Secret Rite buff enhances these bonuses further.
- Elemental Skill provides flat energy, making his Burst cost manageable.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="bxG6N82s_Lk" title="ULTIMATE Durin Guide! [Artifacts, Weapons, Teams, MORE] Genshin Impact" />

Durin's Dark Form, activated by using a Normal Attack after his Elemental Skill, grants a substantial 40% bonus to Vaporize and Melt damage. This bonus can be further increased to 70% with the Hexerei Secret Rite buff, making him a formidable on-field or quick-swap Vaporize DPS. His Elemental Burst in Dark Form also features multiple Pyro ticks, allowing for numerous reaction opportunities.

<Callout type="tip">
To maximize Dark Durin's Vaporize damage, ensure continuous Hydro application on enemies. Characters like Xingqiu or Yelan are excellent choices for this role, as they provide off-field Hydro.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Ensure Hydro Application">
    The cornerstone of any Vape team is consistent Hydro. Characters like Xingqiu and Yelan are ideal for their off-field abilities, allowing Durin to trigger Vaporize frequently with his Elemental Burst ticks. For on-field Vape setups, Mona's Omen can significantly amplify Durin's damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Hexerei Buffs">
    Pairing Durin with another Hexerei character (e.g., Mona, Albedo) is highly recommended. This activates the Hexerei Secret Rite buff, boosting Dark Durin's personal Vaporize and Melt damage by an additional 30%.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Consider Anemo Support">
    An Anemo character like Sucrose or Kazuha can provide Elemental Mastery buffs and Viridescent Venerer resistance shred, further increasing Vaporize damage. Sucrose is particularly good for distributing EM.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optional: On-Field DPS with C6 Bennett">
    For a unique on-field **Durin Vape Team** experience, a C6 Bennett can infuse Durin's Normal Attacks with Pyro, allowing him to trigger Vaporize consistently. This requires precise rotation and Hydro application.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Team Flexibility">
While Hexerei buffs are strong, Durin is still a very solid character even without a second Hexerei member. His off-field Pyro application and resistance shred remain valuable in many compositions.
</Callout>
