Durin, a 5-star Pyro Sword character, stands out for his dual-mode playstyle, offering both robust support and significant damage. His Elemental Skill allows him to switch between "Confirmation of Purity" (White Mode) and "Denial of Darkness" (Dark Mode), fundamentally altering his role within **Durin Genshin teams**. In White Mode, Durin's Elemental Burst provides consistent off-field AoE Pyro application and shreds the resistance of Pyro and other elements involved in reactions (excluding Vaporize and Melt). This makes him an excellent enabler for teams focused on Overload, Burning, Pyro Swirl, or Crystallize. Conversely, Dark Mode empowers his personal Vaporize and Melt damage, transforming him into a potent sub-DPS or even an on-field carry, especially with C6 Bennett. The presence of another Hexerei character further amplifies these effects, boosting RES shred in White Mode and personal reaction damage in Dark Mode.

<Callout type="tip" title="Hexerei Resonance Advantage">
Pairing Durin with another Hexerei character, such as Fischl or Albedo, significantly enhances his passive abilities. This boost can increase his RES shred from 20% to 35% in White Mode, and his personal Vaporize/Melt damage bonus from 40% to 70% in Dark Mode. Prioritize this synergy when building **Durin Genshin teams**.
</Callout>

### Investment Recommendations

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Talent Priority">
    Prioritize **Elemental Burst** first for maximum damage contribution, followed by **Elemental Skill** for increased damage and energy generation. Normal Attacks are only necessary if playing on-field DPS with C6 Bennett.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Constellation Sweet Spots">
    **C0** Durin is already very strong. **C2** is an excellent early stopping point, providing a universal 50% elemental damage bonus to the team. **C6** offers a massive damage spike through DEF shred/ignore.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Leveling to 90">
    Leveling Durin to 90 can provide a noticeable damage increase (around 10% overall). As a versatile sub-DPS, support, or even main DPS, he's a strong candidate for full investment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="C1 vs. Signature Weapon (R1)">
Generally, Durin's C1 offers a more consistent and often larger damage increase for the team compared to his R1 signature weapon, especially if you already have other strong sword options. However, if you lack a good weapon, R1 can still be a valuable acquisition.
