Durin's kit revolves around his Elemental Skill, *Binary Form: Convergence and Division*. By pressing the skill twice, he enters the **White Mode**, which grants his Burst AoE Pyro application and 20% to 35% RES shred. Conversely, using a Normal Attack after the initial Skill cast triggers **Dark Mode**, increasing his personal Vaporize and Melt damage by 40% to 70%.

| Feature | White Mode (Purity) | Dark Mode (Darkness) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Role** | Support / Off-field Sub-DPS | Main Carry / Reaction DPS |
| **Burst Type** | AoE Pyro (1 hit/sec) | Single Target (1 hit/1.25 sec) |
| **Key Buff** | RES Shred (Pyro/Electro/Geo/Animo) | Vape/Melt Damage Bonus |
| **Ideal Reactions** | Overload, Burning, Swirl | Vaporize, Melt |
| **Uptime** | Over 100% (20s duration) | Consistent High Damage |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Mode Tip">
Durin defaults to White Mode if you use his Burst immediately without using his Skill first. This is a great time-saver for speed-clearing Abyss floors where energy is already full.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Premium Rotation & Execution Guide">

Executing a high-end **Durin premium team** rotation requires precision in how you trigger his Binary Form. Mis-timing the skill can result in losing the 35% RES shred for your main carry.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Setup & Buffs">
    Start with your primary buffer (e.g., Bennett Burst or Furina Skill). Ensure all team-wide buffs are active before Durin takes the field.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Durin's Binary Entry">
    - **For White Mode:** Use Skill -> Skill -> Burst. This ensures AoE Pyro and RES shred.
    - **For Dark Mode:** Use Skill -> Normal Attack -> Burst. This locks in the Vape/Melt damage bonus.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Particle Funneling">
    Immediately after Durin's Burst, swap to your main DPS (like Arlecchino or Kinich). The flat energy refund happens instantly, but the particles generated by the skill can be caught by the next character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Main DPS Window">
    Execute your primary attacker's rotation. Durin's off-field Burst will last for 20 seconds, covering even the longest DPS windows (like Cyno or Raiden).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Advanced Tech">
You can "Dash Cancel" or "Jump Cancel" Durin's charged attacks if playing him on-field with C6 Bennett. This significantly increases his frame-data efficiency and Pyro application rate.
</Callout>
