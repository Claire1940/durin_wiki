<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Ascension Materials and Regional Specialty Locations">

Ascending Durin to his final phase requires materials exclusive to the Nod-Krai region. The primary local specialty is the **Frostlamp Flower**, a glowing flora that blooms near water sources and historical sites. These flowers follow a 48-hour respawn timer, making it vital to start your **Durin pre farm** routes at least a week before his banner launch.

| Material | Amount | Location / Enemy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Frostlamp Flower** | 168 | Final Night Cemetery / Frostmoon Enclave |
| **Cyclic Military Kuuvahki Core** | 46 | Super-Heavy Landrover: Mechanized Fortress |
| **Tattered Warrant** | 18 | Fatui Oprichniki (All Levels) |
| **Immaculate Warrant** | 30 | Fatui Oprichniki (Level 40+) |
| **Frostetched Warrant** | 36 | Fatui Oprichniki (Level 60+) |

The Fatui Oprichniki are elite soldiers found patrolling the outskirts of Nod-Krai. Unlike standard Fatui skirmishers, these enemies have higher resistance to physical damage and frequently use elemental shields. Using an interactive map is highly recommended to track their clusters near the Eye of Kratti and the southern banks of Blue Amber Lake.

  <Step num="1" title="Frostlamp Flower Route">

  <Step num="2" title="Fatui Oprichniki Hunting">

<Section icon={Target} title="Talent Leveling and Weekly Boss Schedule">

Maximizing Durin's combat effectiveness requires a massive amount of talent materials. He utilizes the **Ballad** series of talent books, which are found in Mondstadt's **Forsaken Rift** domain. Because these books are only available on specific days, missing a farming window can set your progression back significantly.

| Talent Level | Ballad Books | Warrant Drops | Weekly / Crown |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 2-6** | 9 Teachings / 63 Guide | 18 Tattered / 66 Immaculate | 0 |
| **Level 7-10** | 114 Philosophy | 93 Frostetched | 6 Sunfire / 1 Crown |
| **Total (Per Talent)** | 3 Teachings / 21 Guide / 38 Philosophy | 6 / 22 / 31 | 6 Sunfire / 1 Crown |
| **Total (Triple Crown)** | 9 / 63 / 114 | 18 / 66 / 93 | 18 Sunfire / 3 Crowns |

The **Eroded Sunfire** is a rare drop from the **Lord of Eroded Primal Fire** located in the Stone Stele Records trounce domain. Since weekly bosses only provide rewards once per week, it is crucial to use **Dream Solvent** to convert other drops from the same boss if the Sunfire does not drop naturally.

<Callout type="success" title="Farming Calendar">
Set a reminder for **Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday**. These are the only days the Forsaken Rift domain provides the Ballad books needed for Durin's talents.
</Callout>
