  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Warrant Drops">
    Common enemy drops, the Warrant series, are shared between character and talent ascensions. These are obtained by defeating Fatui Oprichniki soldiers found throughout Nod-Krai.
    <Callout type="info">
      Different tiers of Warrants (Tattered, Immaculate, Frostetched) drop from various levels of Oprichniki. Farm higher-level enemies for better drops.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Obtain Eroded Sunfire">
    As mentioned, Eroded Sunfire is a weekly boss drop from the Lord of Eroded Primal Fire. You'll need 18 pieces for all three talents (Normal Attack, Elemental Skill, Elemental Burst) to Level 10.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure Crowns of Insight">
    Crowns of Insight are rare materials needed for the final talent levels (Level 10). You'll need three to max out all of Durin's talents. These are typically acquired from limited-time events, the Frostbearing Tree, or the Sacred Sakura.
  </Step>
</Steps>
