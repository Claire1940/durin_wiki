<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Farming Ballad Talent Books">
Talent books are a cornerstone of Durin's talent progression. He utilizes the Ballad series, which can be acquired from a specific domain on certain days of the week. Efficiently farming these books is crucial for rapid talent leveling.

<Callout type="warning" title="Domain Schedule">
The Forsaken Rift Domain is only open on **Wednesdays, Saturdays, and Sundays**. Plan your resin usage accordingly to maximize your runs on these days.
</Callout>

### Forsaken Rift Domain Details

| Domain Name | Location | Available Days | Talent Book Series |
| :---------- | :------- | :------------- | :----------------- |
| **Forsaken Rift** | Springvale, Mondstadt | **Wednesday, Saturday, Sunday** | **Ballad** |

The Forsaken Rift is located south of Mondstadt, near Dragonspine. It's a relatively straightforward domain, but bringing characters with good AoE (Area of Effect) damage can significantly speed up your clear times.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Crowns of Insight and Mora for Talent Upgrades">
Beyond the specific materials, every talent level-up requires a substantial amount of Mora and, for the final levels, Crowns of Insight.

### Mora Requirements

Leveling a single talent from 1 to 10 will cost approximately **1,652,500 Mora**. To max out all three talents, you'll need around **4,957,500 Mora**. This makes Mora a significant bottleneck, so ensure you're consistently farming Ley Line Outcrops or accumulating it through other in-game activities.

### Crowns of Insight

Crowns of Insight are extremely rare and limited resources. You'll need **one Crown of Insight per talent** to reach level 10, meaning a total of **three Crowns of Insight** to max all of Durin's talents.

<Callout type="success" title="How to Get Crowns of Insight">
Crowns of Insight are primarily obtained from:
- Completing offerings (e.g., Frostbearing Tree, Sacred Sakura).
- Participating in limited-time events.
- Certain permanent challenges or quests.

Due to their scarcity, use them wisely on your most-used characters and talents.
</Callout>

</Section>
