<Section icon={Target} title="Farming Routes for Ascension Materials">
Efficiently gathering Durin's ascension materials requires a strategic approach. Knowing where to find each item and planning your routes can save you significant time and resin.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Frostlamp Flowers">
    Frostlamp Flowers are Durin's local specialty and are crucial for his ascension. They are found scattered around Nodcry, particularly between the **Final Night Cemetery** and **Frostmoon Enclave**. These flowers respawn over time, so begin collecting them early. You'll need a total of **168**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat Super-Heavy Landrover: Mechanized Fortress">
    The **Cyclic Military Kuuvahki Core** is a new boss material introduced in Version 6.2. This material drops from the **Super-Heavy Landrover: Mechanized Fortress** field boss in Nodcry. This boss will be available upon the release of Version 6.2, so save your **Fragile Resin** for it. You will need **46** cores.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Hunt Fatui Oprichniki">
    The various **Warrant** drops (Tattered, Immaculate, Frostetched) are obtained by defeating **Fatui Oprichniki** soldiers across Nodcry. These common enemy drops are used for both character and talent ascension, so you'll need a significant amount.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Farm Agnidus Agate Gems">
    Agnidus Agate gems can be farmed from any Pyro-aligned world boss, such as the **Pyro Regisvine** or **Pyro Hypostasis**. Alternatively, you can craft them using the Alchemy Bench if you have other elemental gems and Dust of Azoth.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Time-Sensitive Materials">
**Frostlamp Flowers** and **Ballad Talent Books** are time-gated. Flowers have a respawn timer, and talent books are only available on specific days. Prioritize these items first to avoid delays in Durin's progression.
</Callout>

</Section>
