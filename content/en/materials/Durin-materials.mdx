<Section icon={Map} title="Farming Routes and Locations">

Gathering **Durin materials** efficiently requires knowledge of the Nod-Krai geography. The region is divided into several sub-areas where specific enemies and specialties congregate.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Frostlamp Flower Collection">
    Travel to the **Final Night Cemetery** and the **Frostmoon Enclave** in Nod-Krai. These flowers emit a soft blue glow and are typically found near ancient ruins. They take 48 hours to respawn.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Fatui Oprichniki Hunting">
    These soldiers are scattered throughout the snowy plains of Nod-Krai. Use your Adventurer's Handbook to track them. They drop Tattered, Immaculate, and Frost-Etched Warrants.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Super-Heavy Landrover Boss">
    The world boss, **Super-Heavy Landrover: Mechanized Fortress**, is located in a central arena within Nod-Krai. Ensure you bring a character capable of breaking mechanical shields to speed up the fight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ballad Talent Books">
    Visit the **Forsaken Rift** domain in Mondstadt. It is located south of Springvale. Remember to bring Cryo-resistant food or characters, as the domain features a stamina-draining cold aura.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Exploration Bonus">
Durin has a passive talent that grants **25% more rewards** when dispatched on a Mondstadt Expedition for 20 hours. Use this to stock up on extra resources while you farm his primary materials.
</Callout>
