<Section icon={Target} title="Local Specialty: Frostlamp Flower Routes">

The **Frostlamp Flower** is a rare flora found exclusively in the frigid zones of Nod-Krai. These flowers are concentrated in two primary locations: the **Final Night Cemetery** and the **Frostmoon Enclave**. Because they are a local specialty, they only respawn every **48 hours** (real-world time). If you are in a hurry to reach Level 90, you may need to visit the worlds of friends or use the Co-op matching system.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Final Night Cemetery Loop">
    Start at the southern waypoint of the Cemetery. Move in a clockwise circle around the perimeter. These flowers often hide behind stone monuments and under withered trees.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Frostmoon Enclave Descent">
    Teleport to the high cliffs of the Enclave. Glide down toward the frozen lake. Most Frostlamp Flowers grow near the water's edge where the ice meets the shoreline.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Oprichniki Cleanup">
    While gathering flowers, you will encounter **Fatui Oprichniki**. Defeat them immediately to collect the Tattered, Immaculate, and Frostetched Warrants required for both ascension and talents.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Botanist's Secret">
Equip a character with a "Local Specialty Radar" passive (like Tighnari or Lyney, depending on the region's classification) to see the Frostlamp Flower icons on your mini-map. This significantly reduces the time spent searching in the dark terrain of Nod-Krai.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Talent Level-Up & Weekly Boss Materials">

To push Durin's damage to the limit, you must focus on the **Ballad series** talent books and the **Eroded Sunfire** weekly drop. The Ballad books are found in the **Forsaken Rift** domain in Mondstadt. It is one of the older domains, featuring a Cryo aura that drains stamina, so bring a dedicated healer or a Pyro shielder like Thoma or Bennett to mitigate the debuffs.

For the high-level talent ranks (7 through 10), you will need the **Eroded Sunfire**. This is obtained by defeating the **Lord of Eroded Primal Fire** within the **Stone Stele Records** Trounce Domain in Natlan. You can only claim rewards from this boss once per week.
