<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding Durin's Dual Forms">
Durin, the newest 5-star Pyro Sword character in Genshin Impact, offers unparalleled versatility through his unique dual forms: **Confirmation of Purity (White Mode)** and **Denial of Darkness (Dark Mode)**. Each mode fundamentally alters his Elemental Burst and passive abilities, allowing him to adapt to diverse team compositions and combat scenarios. Mastering both forms is crucial for optimizing your **Durin Genshin build**.

Upon using his Elemental Skill, you can choose to enter either mode. Pressing the Elemental Skill button again activates White Mode, while a Normal Attack after the skill triggers Dark Mode. This choice dictates his role in the team, from a potent off-field support to a self-sufficient damage dealer.

**Video Highlights:**
- Durin's Elemental Skill allows mode switching (White Mode: Skill again; Dark Mode: Normal Attack).
- White Mode provides continuous AoE Pyro application and resistance shred.
- Dark Mode focuses on single-target damage with increased Vaporize/Melt potency.
- His skill also generates significant flat energy (30-33 at Talent Level 9-10).

### White Mode: Off-field Support & Sub-DPS
In White Mode, Durin's Elemental Burst, "Dragon of White Flame," provides continuous off-field AoE Pyro application, dealing damage once per second and applying Pyro every second hit. This mode shines in its ability to shred resistances. When Durin triggers Pyro-related reactions (Overload, Pyro Swirl, Pyro Crystallize, Burning, or Dendro Burning), he shreds the resistance of both Pyro and the involved element by 20% (up to 35% with Hexerei resonance). This makes him an exceptional support for teams relying on these reactions.

### Dark Mode: On-field or Off-field Carry
Conversely, Dark Mode's Elemental Burst, "Dragon of Dark Decay," delivers single-target Pyro damage with higher scaling, though at a slower rate (once every 1.25 seconds). In this mode, Durin gains increased damage for Vaporize and Melt reactions (40% base, up to 70% with Hexerei resonance). Dark Mode transforms him into a formidable personal damage dealer, ideal for teams centered around Hydro or Cryo auras.

<Callout type="warning">
White Mode's resistance shred does **not** apply to Vaporize or Melt reactions, as these reactions involve Hydro or Cryo. For these reactions, Dark Mode's personal damage bonus is generally preferred.
</Callout>

<Callout type="success">
Aim for at least **2000 ATK** (factoring in team buffs) to maximize Durin's A4 passive, which significantly increases his Burst damage.
</Callout>

### Sub-Stat Priority

1.  **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG**: Essential for maximizing all damage. Maintain a healthy 1:2 ratio (e.g., 70% CRIT Rate, 140% CRIT DMG).
2.  **ATK%**: Highly valuable, especially up to 2500 effective ATK.
3.  **Energy Recharge**: Durin's Elemental Skill provides significant energy refund.
    -   **0-110% ER**: In teams with strong battery characters (e.g., Fischl, Bennett, other Pyro units) or when his skill is crowned.
    -   **110-140% ER**: For solo Pyro scenarios or less favorable energy generation.
4.  **Elemental Mastery**: Crucial for reaction-focused builds (Burgeon, Vaporize, Melt).
