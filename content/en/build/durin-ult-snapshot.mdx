<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Durin's Elemental Burst">
Durin, a 5-star Pyro Sword character in Genshin Impact, possesses an Elemental Burst that forms the core of his kit. This Burst allows him to deal significant off-field Pyro damage, either focusing on team buffs in his Purity (White) state or personal damage in his Darkness (Dark) state. A crucial aspect of maximizing Durin's damage output is understanding how his Burst interacts with buffs, specifically whether it "snapshots" or not.

**Snapshotting** refers to a game mechanic where an ability's damage or effects are fixed based on the character's stats at the moment of activation. If an ability snapshots, any buffs gained *after* its cast will not influence its ongoing damage. Conversely, if an ability does *not* snapshot, its damage is dynamically recalculated with current buffs. For Durin, his Elemental Burst falls into the latter category.

This means that any buffs applied to Durin *after* he casts his Burst will not affect the damage dealt by the ongoing Burst effect. All buffs must be active *before* his Burst is cast to take effect.

<Section icon={Target} title="How Snapshotting Works in Genshin Impact">
Snapshotting is a fundamental concept for optimizing character damage in Genshin Impact. Abilities that snapshot record a character's stats (like ATK, Elemental DMG Bonus, CRIT stats) at the moment of casting and maintain those stats for the entire duration of the ability. Abilities that do not snapshot recalculate damage in real-time as buffs come and go.

<Callout type="tip">
Generally, if a buff causes a visible change in a character's stat menu (e.g., ATK buffs, Elemental DMG Bonus buffs), it is often snapshottable. Buffs that do not show a visible stat change (e.g., Elemental Burst DMG Bonus, Additive Base DMG Bonus) are typically non-snapshottable and apply in real-time.
</Callout>

### Snapshotting vs. Non-Snapshotting Abilities

Here’s a comparison of common ability types and their snapshotting behavior:

| Ability Type | Snapshotting Behavior | Examples |
| :----------- | :-------------------- | :------- |
| **Summoned Entities** | Often snapshots | Xiangling's Pyronado, Fischl's Oz, Albedo's Solar Isotoma |
| **Persistent Field Effects** | Varies, often real-time | Bennett's Fantastic Voyage (buffs snapshot, but field itself is real-time), Durin's Burst |
| **Single-Instance Damage** | Always real-time | Most Elemental Skill/Burst initial hits |
| **Additive Base DMG Buffs** | Always real-time | Shenhe's Icy Quill, Yun Jin's Burst |

For Durin, his Burst's periodic damage instances are continuously updated. This means a buff like Bennett's ATK buff needs to be active *when each hit lands*, not just when Durin casts his Burst.
