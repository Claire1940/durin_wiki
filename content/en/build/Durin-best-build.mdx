<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Durin Kit Mechanics: White vs. Dark Mode">

Durin is a versatile 5-star Pyro Sword user released in Version 6.2. His gameplay revolves around the **Binary Form** mechanic. After using his Elemental Skill, the next input determines his form for the next 30 seconds. Pressing the Skill again enters **Confirmation of Purity (White Mode)**, while performing a Normal Attack enters **Denial of Darkness (Dark Mode)**.

The choice between modes fundamentally changes his Elemental Burst, **Principle of Purity/Darkness**. In White Mode, he acts as a premier off-field support, applying Pyro every second and shredding resistances. In Dark Mode, he transforms into a front-loaded damage dealer with enhanced Vaporize and Melt multipliers.

| Mechanic | White Mode (Purity) | Dark Mode (Darkness) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Activation** | Skill > Skill | Skill > Normal Attack |
| **Burst Type** | AOE Pyro (21 Ticks) | Single-Target (16 Ticks) |
| **Primary Buff** | 20-35% RES Shred | 40-70% Vape/Melt DMG |
| **Best For** | Overload, Burning, Mono-Pyro | Vaporize, Melt Carry |
| **Application** | 1.0s interval | 1.25s interval |

<Callout type="warning" title="Rotation Priority">
Durin's Burst does **not snapshot**. This means buffs like Bennett's Circle or Noblesse Oblige must remain active throughout his Burst duration to provide their benefits. Always ensure your team buffs are refreshed before swapping Durin out.
</Callout>

For main stats, Durin typically follows the **ATK / Pyro / Crit** template. However, his high energy refund from the Elemental Skill means you can often ignore Energy Recharge (ER) entirely if he is paired with another Pyro unit or Fischl.
