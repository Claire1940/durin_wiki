<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Durin Mechanics and Stat Overview">

Durin is a highly versatile 5-star Pyro Sword character introduced in version 6.2 (Luna III). His kit revolves around two distinct binary states: **Confirmation of Purity (White form)** and **Denial of Darkness (Dark form)**. Choosing the right **Durin artifact stats** depends heavily on which form you intend to utilize. The White form excels as a Sub-DPS and support, providing up to 35% RES shred for Pyro and associated elements. Conversely, the Dark form transforms Durin into a potent reaction carry, gaining a massive 70% damage bonus to Vaporize and Melt when paired with Hexerei teammates.

The most critical aspect of his build is the **Ascension 4 Passive: Chaos Formed Like the Night**. This talent grants a damage multiplier increase of 3% for every 100 ATK, capping at a 75% increase once Durin reaches 2,500 ATK. This makes ATK% stats almost as valuable as Crit, as you are "double-dipping" into both base scaling and a unique motion value multiplier.

<Section icon={Target} title="Main Stat and Substat Optimization">

Finding the perfect balance of **Durin artifact stats** requires looking at his effective ATK and Crit ratio simultaneously. Because Durin gains Crit DMG upon ascension, a Crit Rate circlet is almost always required to maintain a 1:2 ratio.

| Artifact Slot | Main Stat (Support/Sub-DPS) | Main Stat (Carry/Burgeon) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sands** | ATK% | ATK% or Elemental Mastery |
| **Goblet** | Pyro DMG Bonus | Pyro DMG Bonus or ATK% |
| **Circlet** | Crit Rate / Crit DMG | Crit Rate / Crit DMG |

**Substat Priority List:**
1. **Energy Recharge**: Until team requirements are met (typically 110% - 130%).
2. **Crit Rate / Crit DMG**: Aim for a 70/140 or higher ratio.
3. **ATK%**: Highly valuable until hitting the 2,500 effective ATK cap.
4. **Elemental Mastery**: High priority for Dark form; lower for White form.
5. **Flat ATK**: Minor benefit compared to percentage-based stats.

<Callout type="info" title="Effective ATK Calculation">
"Effective ATK" includes all buffs like Bennett's Burst, Noblesse Oblige, and Tenacity of the Millelith. If your team provides 800 ATK in buffs, your Durin only needs 1,700 sheet ATK to maximize his passive.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Energy Recharge Requirements">

Durin's Energy Recharge (ER) needs are remarkably low compared to other 70-cost Burst characters. This is due to his **Binary Form: Convergence and Division** skill, which refunds a flat amount of energy (30 at level 9, 33 at level 10).

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Crown the Elemental Skill">
    Leveling the skill to 10 reduces the effective cost of his Burst from 70 to 37. This is the single most important step for artifact efficiency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Team Resonance">
    In double-Pyro or Overload teams with Fischl, Durin can often run **0% ER from artifacts** and still burst on cooldown.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Solo Pyro Adjustments">
    If Durin is the lone Pyro unit in a team (e.g., Neuvillette or Kinich teams), aim for **130% - 150% ER** to ensure consistency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Team Archetype | Typical ER Requirement | Recommended Artifact Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Overload (w/ Chevreuse)** | 100% - 110% | Pure Offensive Stats |
| **Double Pyro (w/ Bennett)** | 100% - 115% | Pure Offensive Stats |
| **Solo Pyro Support** | 130% - 145% | ER Substats or ER Sands |
| **Burgeon Carry** | 120% - 140% | EM Mainstats + ER Substats |

<Callout type="success" title="Rotation Tip">
The energy refund triggers whenever Durin enters a state. You can cast Skill -> Burst -> Skill (second cast) to catch particles and trigger the refund without wasting energy if your meter was already full.
</Callout>

</Section>
