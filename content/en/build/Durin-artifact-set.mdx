<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding Durin's Core Mechanics">
Durin, a 5-star Pyro Sword character in Genshin Impact, offers immense versatility through his dual-mode combat system. He can function as an off-field Burst DPS, a powerful support, or even an on-field damage dealer. His effectiveness is deeply tied to how players leverage his White Mode (support/sub-DPS with RES shred) and Dark Mode (personal damage with enhanced reaction buffs). Understanding these modes is crucial for selecting the best **Durin artifact set**.

**Video Highlights:**
- Durin has two distinct modes: White (support/sub-DPS) and Dark (personal DPS).
- White Mode provides AoE Pyro application and RES shred for various reactions.
- Dark Mode offers single-target Pyro damage and increased Vaporize/Melt damage.
- His Elemental Skill generates significant energy, reducing his ER requirements.

Durin's Elemental Skill allows him to switch between these forms and also provides a substantial energy refund, making his Energy Recharge needs relatively low compared to other characters. This flexibility means that his optimal artifact set can vary significantly based on the team composition and intended playstyle.

<Callout type="tip">
Durin's A1 Passive is enhanced by 75% when paired with another Hexerei character. In White Mode, this means 35% RES Shred, and in Dark Mode, it translates to a 70% Melt/Vaporize DMG Bonus. Prioritizing a Hexerei teammate can greatly increase his artifact set's effectiveness.
</Callout>
