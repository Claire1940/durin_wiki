<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Understanding Durin's Energy Recharge Mechanics">
Durin, a versatile Pyro 5-star character in Genshin Impact, possesses a unique Elemental Skill that significantly impacts his Energy Recharge (ER) requirements. His Elemental Burst, while powerful, typically costs 70 energy. However, his skill's ability to generate flat energy can drastically reduce this effective cost, making his ER needs highly flexible and team-dependent.

**Video Highlights:**
- Durin's Burst initially costs 70 energy.
- His Elemental Skill generates 33 flat energy at Talent Level 10 (42 at C5/Talent 13).
- This flat energy effectively lowers his Burst cost to 37 energy at Talent Level 10.
- Energy generation is instant and does not require hitting an enemy.
- The flat energy generation has a 6-second cooldown.

Durin's Elemental Skill generates 4 Pyro particles upon hitting an enemy, in addition to the flat energy refund. This dual energy generation mechanism makes him less reliant on external energy sources in many team compositions.

<Callout type="tip">
Leveling Durin's Elemental Skill to at least Level 10 is highly recommended. This significantly reduces his effective Elemental Burst cost, allowing for much lower Energy Recharge requirements and freeing up artifact substats for more offensive stats like Crit and ATK%.
</Callout>

    - **Two Skill Casts**: If Durin can cast his Elemental Skill twice per rotation, he may need virtually no ER.

  <Card title="Higher ER Scenarios">

    - **Low Particle Generation**: Teams with limited energy support (e.g., Durin, Fischl, Albedo, Mona) can push his ER needs to **160% or higher**.
    - **Off-field with 0 Pyro Teammates**: 120-150% ER is a good range.
  </Card>
