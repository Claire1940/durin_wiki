Durin is a highly versatile 5-star Pyro Sword character released in the Luna III (6.2) update. His kit revolves around a unique dual-state mechanic that dictates his role in a team. Selecting the **Durin best artifacts** depends heavily on whether you intend to utilize his supportive White Mode or his carry-oriented Dark Mode. Unlike many Pyro units, Durin provides massive off-field utility, boasting nearly 100% Burst uptime with an 18-second cooldown and 20-second duration.

After using his Elemental Skill, Durin can enter one of two states. Re-casting the skill triggers **White Mode**, which allows his Burst to shred Pyro and elemental resistances (up to 35% with Hexerei resonance). Performing a Normal Attack instead enters **Dark Mode**, shifting his focus to personal damage by increasing Vaporize and Melt DMG by up to 70%.

<Callout type="tip" title="Energy Tip">
Durin's Skill restores 30 Energy at Talent Level 9. Because his Burst costs 70, this refund effectively reduces his "real" cost to 40, making Energy Recharge substats a low priority in most double-Pyro or Overload teams.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Artifact Stat Priorities and Thresholds">

To optimize the **Durin best artifacts**, you must pay attention to his A4 passive: **Primordial Fusion**. This talent increases his Burst damage by 3% for every 100 ATK he possesses, capping at a 75% increase at **2500 ATK**. This makes ATK% stats almost as valuable as Crit until the cap is reached.

| Slot | Main Stat | Priority Sub-stats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sands** | ATK% (or EM for Vape) | Crit Rate / Crit DMG |
| **Goblet** | Pyro DMG% or ATK% | ATK% / Energy Recharge |
| **Circlet** | Crit Rate / Crit DMG | Elemental Mastery |

**Energy Recharge (ER) Requirements:**
- **Double Pyro / Overload**: 100% - 110% (No ER needed).
- **Solo Pyro (Off-field)**: 130% - 150%.
- **On-field DPS**: 100% - 120%.

<Callout type="warning" title="Stat Saturation">
While ATK is vital for Durin, do not ignore Crit. Once you reach 2500 ATK through team buffs (like Bennett or Noblesse), additional ATK provides diminishing returns compared to Crit Damage.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Team Building">

Building a team around Durin requires deciding on your primary reaction. His Hexerei passive makes him a natural partner for characters like **Fischl**, **Albedo**, and **Sucrose**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose Your Mode">
    Decide between **White Mode** (to shred resistances for an Electro, Anemo, or Geo carry) or **Dark Mode** (to maximize Durin's own Vaporize/Melt damage).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Activate Hexerei Resonance">
    Include at least one other **Hexerei** character. Fischl is the premier choice for Overload, while Albedo works perfectly for Geo or general Sub-DPS teams.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select a Buffer/Healer">
    **Bennett** is Durin's best friend, helping him reach the 2500 ATK cap easily. For Overload teams, **Chevreuse** provides essential resistance shred and DMG buffs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Fill the Carry Slot">
    In White Mode, use a carry like **Clorinde**, **Arlecchino**, or **Kinich**. In Dark Mode, pair Durin with off-field Hydro/Cryo appliers like **Furina** or **Rosaria**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Overload Synergy">
The Durin-Fischl-Chevreuse core is one of the strongest in the 2026 meta. It provides massive Pyro/Electro shred and allows Durin to function as a high-damage off-field battery.
</Callout>
