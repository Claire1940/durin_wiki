### White Form RES Shred Details

In his White Form, known as "Dragon of White Flame," Durin's Ascension 1 Passive, "Light Manifest of the Divine Calculus," is activated. This passive triggers when nearby party members cause certain Pyro-related reactions or deal Pyro/Dendro DMG to enemies affected by Burning.

    - **Affected Elements**: Pyro, Anemo, Dendro, Geo RES.
    - **Reduction**: **20%** for 6 seconds.
    - **Unaffected**: Hydro and Cryo RES.

<Section icon={Target} title="Rotation and Energy Management for Consistent RES Shred">
Maintaining consistent uptime on Durin's RES shred requires careful rotation and energy management. His Elemental Skill plays a crucial role in both energy generation and form switching.

### Durin's Elemental Skill and Burst Mechanics

| Talent | Description | RES Shred Relevance |
| :----- | :---------- | :------------------ |
| **Elemental Skill: Binary Form** | Replaces with special versions: Confirmation of Purity (Light) or Denial of Darkness (Dark). Generates 4 Pyro particles and restores energy (up to 33 flat energy at Talent Lvl 9). | Essential for entering White Form to activate RES shred. Energy refund helps maintain Burst uptime. |
| **Elemental Burst: Principle of Purity** | (White Form) Deals AoE Pyro DMG, then attacks nearby opponents every 1s. Has 20s uptime, 18s cooldown. | The Burst itself triggers the A1 Passive RES shred upon activation and subsequent hits. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Burning Aura Anti-Synergy">
In pure Burning teams, Durin's RES shred can be difficult to maintain. Once an enemy has a Burning aura, the reaction cannot be re-triggered, causing the RES shred to expire unless a new enemy appears or the aura is cleansed. This makes consistent **Durin RES shred** challenging in such specific Burning-focused compositions without careful management.
</Callout>

### Recommended Rotation for White Form RES Shred

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Skill Cast">
    Cast Durin's Elemental Skill to enter the **Confirmation of Purity (White Form)**. This immediately grants energy and prepares his supportive Burst.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Activate Hexerei Teammate Buffs">
    Swap to your Hexerei teammate (e.g., Fischl, Albedo) to apply their buffs and set up elemental reactions, ensuring they are active before Durin's Burst.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unleash Elemental Burst">
    Immediately use Durin's Elemental Burst. This activates his A1 Passive, applying the **20% (or 35% with Hexerei)** RES shred to affected enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Main DPS On-Field Rotation">
    Switch to your main on-field DPS character to capitalize on the applied RES shred and any other buffs from your Hexerei teammate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Re-apply Elemental Skill (if needed)">
    If Durin's Burst is fully charged and you need to re-enter White Form or generate more particles, cast his Skill again after his Burst.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Durin's low energy requirements (120-150% ER in solo Pyro, 100-110% in double Pyro) mean he can often cast his Burst consistently, ensuring high uptime on his valuable RES shred.

### Additional Utility and Constellations

Durin also provides other valuable utility:
- **Energy Regeneration**: His Elemental Skill restores a significant amount of flat energy, reducing his and potentially his team's ER requirements.
- **Faction Buffs**: As a Hexerei character, he benefits from and enables faction buffs, increasing the value of other Hexerei teammates.

His constellations further enhance his capabilities:
- **C1 (Adamah's Redemption)**: Provides an additive Base DMG Bonus to either the active character (White Form) or Durin's Burst (Dark Form).
- **C2 (Unground Visions)**: Increases all nearby party members' Pyro DMG and corresponding elemental DMG by 50% for 6s after triggering reactions. This directly amplifies the impact of his RES shred by boosting the damage of affected elements.
- **C6 (Dual Birth)**: Durin's Burst DMG ignores 30% of opponents' DEF, and in White Form, it further decreases opponent DEF by 30% for 6s. This is a significant personal damage and support boost.
