Durin is a revolutionary 5-star Pyro Sword character released in the Luna III (6.2) update. Unlike traditional units, his playstyle is dictated by the **Essential Transmutation** state triggered by his Elemental Skill. He functions as the premier Pyro off-fielder for the 2026 meta, offering either massive resistance shred or explosive personal reaction damage.

- **White Mode**: Focuses on AoE Pyro application and shredding Elemental RES for the team.

- **Energy Refund**: At Talent Level 9, entering a state refunds 30 Energy, effectively lowering his Burst cost from 70 to 40.
