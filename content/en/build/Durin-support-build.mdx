<Section icon={Map} title="Core Mechanics: Mastering the White Mode">

The foundation of a successful **Durin support build** lies in understanding his dual-mode system. Durin enters the battlefield in a neutral state, but his Elemental Skill, **Binary Form: Convergence and Division**, allows him to transmute into either the **Confirmation of Purity** (White Mode) or the **Denial of Darkness** (Dark Mode). For a support-oriented playstyle, White Mode is non-negotiable. 

In this state, Durin's Elemental Burst manifests the **Dragon of White Flame**. This entity follows your active character for 20 seconds, dealing AoE Pyro damage every second. More importantly, it triggers his A1 Passive, shredding the Pyro RES and the RES of the element involved in any Pyro reaction (Overload, Swirl, Crystallize, or Burning) by 20%. When the **Hexerei: Secret Rite** resonance is active, this shred jumps to a massive 35%, making Durin a premier alternative to Viridescent Venerer users.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimal Support Rotation">

To maximize the efficiency of a **Durin support build**, your rotation must ensure that his Burst is active before your main carry takes the field. Because Durin's Burst does not snapshot, he can be swapped in and out without losing effectiveness.

1.  **Skill > Skill**: Immediately cast the second skill to enter White Mode and generate 4 Pyro particles.
2.  **Elemental Burst**: Deploy the Dragon of White Flame. The 20s duration ensures it lasts through your entire team's setup.
3.  **Support Setup**: Swap to other supports (e.g., Chevreuse Skill, Fischl Oz) to trigger reactions and activate the RES shred.
4.  **Main Carry DPS**: Swap to your carry and perform their full rotation.
5.  **Skill (Optional)**: If Durin's Skill is off cooldown and you need energy, swap back briefly before restarting the cycle.

<Callout type="tip" title="Energy Refund Trick">
Durin's Skill restores **30-33 flat energy** upon entering a mode. If your energy is already full, you can cast your **Burst first**, then use the **Skill > Skill** combo to immediately start refilling your meter for the next rotation.
</Callout>
