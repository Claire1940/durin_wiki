<Section icon={Map} title="Core Mechanics: Understanding Binary Forms">

Durin is a versatile 5-star Pyro Sword user whose playstyle shifts dramatically based on his **Binary Form: Convergence and Division**. Unlike traditional characters, Durin's role is determined by the sequence of inputs following his Elemental Skill. Entering the correct form is the foundation of any successful Durin DPS build.

Durin's forms are activated as follows:
- **White Mode (Confirmation of Purity):** Press Elemental Skill twice. This mode provides massive AoE Pyro application and shreds enemy resistances (20% baseline, 35% with Hexerei).
- **Dark Mode (Denial of Darkness):** Press Elemental Skill then immediately follow with a Normal Attack. This mode focuses on single-target damage and increases Vaporize/Melt reaction damage (40% baseline, 70% with Hexerei).

| Mechanic | White Mode (Light) | Dark Mode (Dark) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Trigger** | Skill > Skill | Skill > Normal Attack |
| **Burst Type** | AoE Pyro Hits (1s intervals) | Single-Target Hits (1.25s intervals) |
| **Passive Buff** | RES Shred (Pyro + Swirl/Overload) | Increased Vape/Melt Damage |
| **Role** | Sub-DPS / Resistance Support | On-field or Off-field Carry |
| **Best Synergy** | Chevreuse, Fischl, Kinich | Mona, Furina, Cryo Enablers |

<Callout type="tip" title="Energy Management Tip">
Durin's Elemental Skill refunds a flat amount of energy (30 at Level 9, 33 at Level 10). Because his Burst costs 70 Energy, this refund makes him extremely easy to build with low Energy Recharge (ER), often requiring 0% additional ER in double Pyro or Electro-charged teams.
</Callout>
