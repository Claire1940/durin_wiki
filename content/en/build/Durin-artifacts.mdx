<Section icon={Target} title="Artifact Stat Priority & Thresholds">

Durin scales primarily with **ATK**, but his crit requirements remain standard for a damage dealer. His second passive, **Chaos Formed Like the Night**, provides a massive damage multiplier based on his total ATK, capping at **2500 ATK**.

| Piece | Main Stat | Priority Sub-stats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sands** | ATK% / Elemental Mastery | Crit Rate > Crit DMG > ATK% > ER |
| **Goblet** | Pyro DMG Bonus / ATK% | Crit Rate > Crit DMG > EM > ATK% |
| **Circlet** | Crit Rate / Crit DMG | Crit > ATK% > ER > EM |

**Energy Recharge Thresholds**
Durin's Energy needs are unique because his Skill restores **30 Energy** (at Level 9) or **33 Energy** (at Level 10). This refund happens instantly upon entering a state.

| Team Setup | ER Requirement | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Double Pyro / Overload** | 100% - 110% | Virtually no ER needed if using Skill twice |
| **Solo Pyro Off-Field** | 140% - 160% | Requires some ER sub-stats or Favonius users |
| **On-Field DPS** | 100% - 120% | Bennett and particles usually cover energy |

<Callout type="success" title="The 2500 ATK Goal">
Reaching **2500 effective ATK** (including buffs from Bennett, Noblesse, or weapons) is the most efficient way to scale Durin's damage. Once you hit this mark, prioritize **Crit Rate and Crit DMG** exclusively, as the value of additional ATK begins to diminish compared to crit multipliers.
</Callout>

  <Step num="2" title="Define the Core Reaction">
