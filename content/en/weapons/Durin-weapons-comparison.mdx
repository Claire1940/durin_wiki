<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Durin's Weapon Scaling">

Choosing the right blade in a **Durin weapons comparison** requires understanding his dual-state mechanics. Durin operates in either the **Confirmation of Purity (White Form)** for team buffs and RES shred or the **Denial of Darkness (Dark Form)** for personal Melt and Vaporize damage. Because his Elemental Burst constitutes the vast majority of his output, weapons that buff Burst DMG or provide CRIT stats are highly valued.

His Ascension 4 passive, *Chaos Formed Like the Night*, is a critical factor in weapon selection. It grants a 3% DMG increase for every 100 ATK, capping at 75%. To hit this cap, Durin needs 2500 total ATK. Weapons with high Base ATK or ATK% secondaries help reach this threshold without sacrificing a Pyro DMG goblet for an ATK% one.

| Scaling Factor | Importance | Optimal Target |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base ATK** | Very High | 600+ (5-star) or 510+ (4-star) |
| **CRIT Rate** | High | 70% Minimum |
| **Energy Recharge** | Situational | 120-150% (Solo Pyro) |
| **Elemental Mastery** | Moderate | 100-200 (Vape/Melt teams) |

<Callout type="info" title="Energy Requirements">
If Durin is the solo Pyro unit, his ER needs can jump to 150%. However, in double Pyro teams or with his C5 constellation, he can often run 100% ER, allowing for pure offensive weapon choices.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Top-Tier 5-Star Weapon Rankings">

In any **Durin weapons comparison**, the signature weapon **Athame Artis** sets the gold standard. It provides a massive CRIT Rate secondary and a passive that increases Burst DMG while granting ATK% to the active character. When played with another Hexerei teammate, the efficiency of this weapon increases significantly, making it roughly 10-15% better than the next best 5-star option.

However, players who already own generalist CRIT swords may find the marginal gain of pulling for the signature weapon unnecessary. Weapons like **Primordial Jade Cutter** provide a massive CRIT Rate boost that simplifies artifact farming, while **Mistsplitter Reforged** offers high Elemental DMG bonuses that scale exceptionally well in his Darkness state.

<Section icon={Target} title="Best 4-Star and F2P Sword Options">

For many players, the **Durin weapons comparison** focuses on 4-star accessibility. **Wolf-Fang** is the standout performer here. At R5, it competes directly with low-refinement 5-star swords. It provides CRIT Rate for both the Elemental Skill and Burst, which perfectly aligns with Durin's kit. However, note that its passive stacks cannot be generated while Durin is off-field, requiring him to take a brief moment of field time to maximize the buff.

F2P players should look toward **Moonweaver's Dawn**, obtained through the "Echoes of an Unfinished Past" quest. While it doesn't offer the same ceiling as gacha options, its high ATK substat and Burst DMG bonus make it a reliable fallback.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Evaluate Your Budget">
    If you have the Battle Pass, prioritize **Wolf-Fang**. It is the most consistent 4-star damage dealer for Durin's Darkness state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check Team Comps">
    In Overload or Burning teams, **Lion's Roar (R5)** provides a 36% DMG bonus against enemies affected by Pyro, which is almost always active.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assess Crafting Options">
    If you have a dedicated healer like Bennett or Xilonen, **Finale of the Deep** provides a massive ATK boost that helps hit the A4 passive cap.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Wolf-Fang Limitation">

<Section icon={Map} title="Form-Specific Recommendations">

The **Durin weapons comparison** changes drastically if you play him as a support in the Purity state. In these teams, his personal damage is secondary to his ability to shred RES and buff teammates. **Freedom-Sworn** becomes a top-tier pick here, as it provides a team-wide ATK and Normal/Charged Attack DMG buff.

If the team struggles with Energy, **Favonius Sword** is the best utility choice. It allows Durin to act as a battery for high-cost teammates like Xiangling or Beidou while ensuring his own Burst remains available on every rotation.

| Build Path | Recommended Weapon | Primary Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Support (Purity)** | Freedom-Sworn | Team ATK & DMG Buffs |
| **Utility (Purity)** | Favonius Sword | Team Energy Generation |
| **Vape/Melt (Dark)** | Athame Artis | Maximize Personal DPS |
| **Overvape (Dark)** | Lion's Roar | Reaction Damage Bonus |

<Callout type="tip" title="Dual-Carry Flexibility">
In "Dual-Carry" teams with Neuvillette or Mualani, Durin can cast his Skill twice per rotation. This eliminates his ER requirements, allowing you to use purely offensive weapons like **Mistsplitter Reforged** even without a battery.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Artifact Synergy and Stat Goals">

Weapon choice directly influences your artifact stat priority. If using a weapon with an ATK% substat like **Finale of the Deep**, you will likely need a CRIT Rate circlet to maintain a healthy ratio. Conversely, a weapon like **Primordial Jade Cutter** allows you to focus almost entirely on CRIT DMG and ATK% substats.

For artifact sets, **4pc Noblesse Oblige** is the most common choice for his generalist builds. However, in specific reaction teams, **4pc Crimson Witch of Flames** or **4pc Gilded Dreams** can pull ahead, especially when using weapons that provide Elemental Mastery like **Xiphos' Moonlight**.
