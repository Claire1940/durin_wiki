Durin, a 5-star Pyro Sword character in Genshin Impact, primarily functions as a Hexerei Pyro Sub-DPS. Therefore, selecting the right **Durin 4 star weapon** is crucial for optimizing his performance.

- Elemental Mastery and Elemental Burst Damage Bonus are also valuable stats.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimizing Durin's Kit with 4-Star Weapons">
To get the most out of your chosen **Durin 4 star weapon**, understanding how it synergizes with his abilities and team compositions is key. Durin's dual forms (Light and Dark) can influence weapon effectiveness, especially concerning elemental reactions.

### Recommended Steps for Weapon Optimization

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess Durin's Role in Your Team">
    Determine if Durin is primarily a Sub-DPS for raw damage, a reaction enabler (Vaporize/Melt), or a buffer. This will guide your stat priority. For example, a reaction-focused build might benefit more from Elemental Mastery provided by Iron Sting.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Match Weapon to Artifacts">
    Balance the stats provided by your weapon with your artifact sub-stats. If your chosen **Durin 4 star weapon** offers plenty of CRIT Rate (like Wolf-Fang), you can focus on CRIT DMG or ATK% on your artifacts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Consider Energy Recharge Needs">
    Durin's 70-cost Elemental Burst requires consistent energy. If your team lacks strong Pyro energy generation or you don't have C6 Bennett, a weapon like Amenoma Kageuchi can significantly reduce your Energy Recharge requirements on artifacts. Aim for 110%-140% ER if solo Pyro.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Refine Your Weapon">
    Refining 4-star weapons significantly boosts their passive effects. For example, a fully refined Wolf-Fang (R5) offers substantial damage increases to Durin's Elemental Skill and Burst.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Situational Weapon Choices">
While Wolf-Fang is generally excellent, weapons like Lion's Roar can outperform it in specific team compositions where enemies are consistently affected by Pyro or Electro. Always consider your full team synergy.
</Callout>

</Section>
