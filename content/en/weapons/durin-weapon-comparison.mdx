<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Durin's Forms and Weapon Synergy">

Durin, a 5-star Pyro Sword character in Genshin Impact, excels in off-field Burst damage, with his kit revolving around two distinct states: Light (Purity) and Dark (Darkness). Each form dictates his playstyle, artifact choices, and, most importantly, weapon synergy. Understanding these differences is key to making the optimal **Durin weapon comparison**.

In his **Light Form (Purity)**, Durin focuses on team buffs, providing RES Shred for various elements when certain reactions are triggered. This form benefits from weapons that offer utility, such as Energy Recharge or team-wide buffs, allowing him to support the team effectively while maintaining Burst uptime.

Conversely, his **Dark Form (Darkness)** is geared towards personal damage, significantly boosting his Melt and Vaporize damage. For this form, weapons that enhance CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, and Burst DMG are paramount to maximize his individual output in reactive teams.

When considering vertical investment, Durin's Constellations often provide a more significant DPS increase compared to refining his signature weapon. If you already possess other 5-star CRIT Swords, investing in Durin's Constellations becomes an even more attractive option.

**Athame Artis** is Durin's best-in-slot DPS weapon, providing CRIT Rate as a secondary stat, along with Burst DMG% and ATK% buffs. Its passive effects are further maximized when Durin is paired with at least one other Hexerei character.

<Section icon={Target} title="Weapon Selection Process: A Step-by-Step Guide">

Choosing the right weapon for Durin involves a systematic approach, considering his form, team composition, and your available resources. This process ensures you make the most informed decision for your specific build.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Determine Durin's Primary Role">
    Decide if you primarily want Durin to function as a **team buffer (Light Form)** or a **personal DPS (Dark Form)**. This decision is fundamental as it dictates the stat priorities for his weapon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assess Your Current Weapon Inventory">
    Check your existing 5-star CRIT swords (e.g., Primordial Jade Cutter, Mistsplitter Reforged). If you have one, it might already be a strong option for Dark Form Durin, potentially making his signature weapon a lower priority.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Consider Hexerei Synergy">
    If you plan to run Durin with another Hexerei character, weapons like **Athame Artis** or **Freedom-Sworn** gain significant value due to their enhanced passive effects.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evaluate 4-Star Options and Refinements">
    For 4-star choices, prioritize **Wolf-Fang** (especially at R5) for DPS. For F2P, **Moonweaver's Dawn** is a great accessible option, while **Finale of the Deep** shines in teams with healers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Artifacts and Energy Requirements">
    Ensure your chosen weapon complements your artifact set and helps meet Durin's Energy Recharge requirements. For example, if you lack ER from artifacts, a Favonius Sword might be necessary for Light Form, or Xiphos' Moonlight for EM-focused builds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Artifact Synergy">
Always consider how your chosen weapon interacts with your artifact set. For Dark Form, aim for a balance of CRIT Rate/DMG, ATK%, and EM. For Light Form, focus on ER and ATK% to maximize his supportive capabilities.
