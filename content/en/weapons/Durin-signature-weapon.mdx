<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Passive Mechanics & Hexerei Synergy">

The passive ability of the **Durin signature weapon**, titled **Blade of the Daylight Hours**, is what truly sets it apart. It focuses exclusively on Elemental Burst damage and team-wide utility. The passive provides a flat 16% Crit DMG bonus to Elemental Bursts, which is particularly effective for Durin’s Dark Form, specializing in Vaporize or Melt reactions.

The second part of the passive triggers upon an Elemental Burst hit. It grants a 20% ATK buff to the wielder and a 16% ATK buff to the rest of the team for 3 seconds. While 3 seconds seems short, any character with a persistent Burst (like Durin's Dragon of White Flame) can refresh this timer continuously.

| Effect Component | Base Value | With Hexerei Bonus (+75%) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Burst Crit DMG** | 16% | 16% (No Change) |
| **Wielder ATK Buff** | 20% | **35%** |
| **Team ATK Buff** | 16% | **28%** |
| **Buff Duration** | 3s | 3s (Refreshes on Hit) |

The "Hexerei: Secret Rite" requirement is the most restrictive part of the blade. To maximize the buffs to 35% and 28% respectively, you must have at least two Hexerei characters in the party. Durin himself counts as one, meaning you only need one more (such as Nicole or Albedo) to activate the full potential. According to [Game8's Durin Guide](https://game8.co/games/Genshin-Impact/archives/462478), this makes him the best-in-slot Pyro off-fielder for Overloaded and Burning teams.

<Callout type="info" title="Mechanic Note">
The ATK buffs are calculated based on the character's Base ATK (Character ATK + Weapon ATK). For Durin, who has a high base ATK, this 35% buff results in a massive flat increase.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Comparison: Athame Artis vs. Other Swords">

How does the **Durin signature weapon** stack up against the existing 5-star sword pantheon? In general, it is a "utility-first" weapon. While it doesn't offer the raw DMG% of **Mistsplitter Reforged** or the massive Crit DMG of **Absolution**, its ability to buff the entire team makes it a superior choice for quick-swap and sub-DPS heavy rotations.

| Weapon | Base ATK | Substat | Main Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Athame Artis** | 608 | 33.1% Crit Rate | Team-wide ATK buff + Hexerei synergy |
| **Freedom-Sworn** | 608 | 198 EM | Better for reaction-heavy EM teams |
| **Mistsplitter** | 674 | 44.1% Crit DMG | Higher personal damage for on-field DPS |
| **Wolf-Fang** | 510 | 27.6% Crit Rate | Best 4-star alternative for Burst DMG |

For Durin specifically, Athame Artis is roughly 15-20% better than the next best option (Freedom-Sworn) when the Hexerei bonus is active. Without the Hexerei bonus, the gap closes, and **Primordial Jade Cutter** becomes a competitive alternative due to its massive Crit Rate and HP-to-ATK conversion.

<Callout type="info" title="Final Verdict">
Pull for Athame Artis if you intend to run Durin in his optimal Hexerei teams. If you plan to use him as a generic Pyro slot in non-Hexerei teams, you can safely skip this for a high-refinement Wolf-Fang or a Primordial Jade Cutter.
</Callout>
