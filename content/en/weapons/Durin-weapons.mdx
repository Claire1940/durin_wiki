<Section icon={Target} title="Top 5-Star Weapon Alternatives">

If you do not have access to his signature blade, several other 5-star **Durin weapons** provide excellent "stat-stick" value. Because Durin ascends with CRIT DMG, weapons that provide CRIT Rate are generally easier to build around to maintain a healthy 1:2 ratio.

**Primordial Jade Cutter** is a standout choice due to its massive 44.1% CRIT Rate. Since Durin has no HP scaling, the passive's HP-to-ATK conversion isn't fully maximized, but the raw stats alone make it incredibly competitive. It is particularly strong for Dark Durin builds focusing on on-field Vaporize damage.

**Mistsplitter Reforged** offers high base ATK (674) and CRIT DMG. Durin can easily maintain two stacks of the Elemental DMG bonus by using his Burst and keeping his Energy below 100%. However, getting the third stack requires a Normal Attack to deal Elemental damage, which Durin cannot do without external infusions like C6 Bennett.

<Callout type="info" title="Support Focus">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Stat Priority and Build Requirements">

To optimize any of the **Durin weapons**, your artifact sub-stats must compensate for what the weapon lacks. Durin has a high ATK requirement (2500) to max out his "Chaos Formed Like the Night" passive, which grants a direct damage bonus to his Burst.

**Energy Recharge Targets:**
- **Solo Pyro**: 150% - 170%
- **Double Pyro (with Battery)**: 120% - 140%
- **Triple Pyro/Hexerei**: 110% - 120%

| Stat Type | Priority | Target Value | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Energy Recharge** | High | 130%+ | Critical for Burst uptime |
| **CRIT Rate** | High | 70%+ | Ensures consistent ticks |
| **ATK%** | Medium | 2500 | Maxes out passive bonus |
| **Elemental Mastery** | Low/Med | 100-200 | Only for Dark Durin Vape |

<Callout type="warning" title="The 2500 ATK Cap">
Don't over-invest in ATK beyond 2500 if it means sacrificing CRIT. The passive bonus caps at 2500 ATK; any amount above this only provides standard scaling without the extra multiplier.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="How to Maximize Weapon Performance">

To get the most out of your chosen **Durin weapons**, you must follow a strict rotation to ensure passives are triggered correctly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Trigger Mode Selection">
    Use **Skill > Skill** for White Mode (Support/Shred) or **Skill > Normal Attack** for Dark Mode (DPS). This generates 33-42 flat energy depending on talent level.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Activate Weapon Passives">
    If using **Athame Artis** or **Mistsplitter**, immediately cast your Burst. The first 10 hits of Durin's Burst deal significantly more damage and will benefit from the freshly triggered ATK/Elemental buffs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Off-field Management">
    Swap to your main DPS. Weapons like **Athame Artis** and **Wolf-Fang** (partial) allow Durin to continue buffing or dealing damage while off-field.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Energy Management">
    Catch particles with Durin if you are using lower ER weapons like **Jade Cutter**. His Skill has a 6-second cooldown for flat energy generation—use it even if you don't need the transformation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Quick Swap Tech">
You can skip the Skill transformation and go straight into Burst to default to White Mode. This is ideal for speedrunning when you only need the Pyro application and don't have time for the full 3.1s field time rotation.
</Callout>
