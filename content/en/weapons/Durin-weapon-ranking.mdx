<Section icon={Target} title="Weapon Ascension and Refinement Process">
Ascending and refining Durin's weapons is crucial for maximizing his damage potential. This process involves gathering specific materials and investing resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Ascension Materials">
    Each weapon requires unique materials for ascension, typically found from Elite Enemies, Bosses, and Weapon Material Domains. For example, Athame Artis requires specific weapon materials and common enemy drops. Focus on farming these domains on the correct days.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Refinement Materials">
    Refinement increases a weapon's passive ability. For gacha weapons like Athame Artis, this means obtaining duplicates through the Wish system. For craftable or event weapons, specific refinement materials may be available through quests or events.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Level Up the Weapon">
    Use Weapon Enhancement Materials (Mystic Enhancement Ore, Fine Enhancement Ore, etc.) to level up the weapon to its maximum level for its current ascension phase. This significantly boosts Base ATK and substats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perform Ascension">
    Once the weapon reaches its current max level and you have the required materials, ascend it to unlock higher levels and further stat increases. Repeat this process until the weapon reaches level 90.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Refine to Max (R5)">
    If possible, refine the weapon to its maximum level (R5) to unlock its full passive potential. This often provides a substantial boost to its effectiveness, especially for weapons with powerful passive effects.
  </Step>
</Steps>
