Durin functions primarily as a Pyro Sub-DPS and team buffer, utilizing his unique dual-form mechanics to shred resistances or drive elemental reactions. <Section icon={Shield} title="Budget & F2P Alternatives">

Free-to-play players have several strong options that can be obtained through crafting or early-game exploration. These weapons focus on solving Durin's energy needs or providing accessible stat boosts.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain the Saimon Heirloom">
    Complete the "Farmer's Treasure" world quest in Inazuma to receive the Amenoma Kageuchi blueprint.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Craft Amenoma Kageuchi">
    Use Northlander Sword Billets and Amethyst Chunks to craft this weapon. It provides a massive 55.1% ATK bonus.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Energy Stacks">
    Amenoma's passive generates "Succession Seeds" when using Elemental Skills, which restore energy after casting a Burst.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Refine to R5">
    High refinements on Amenoma almost entirely negate Durin's need for Energy Recharge sub-stats on artifacts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Early Game Recommendation">
If you are below Adventure Rank 45, **Harbinger of Dawn** is your best friend. It provides massive CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG. Just ensure Durin stays above 90% HP to keep the passive active—easy to do since he is mostly an off-field Sub-DPS.
</Callout>
