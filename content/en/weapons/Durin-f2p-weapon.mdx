### Step-by-Step Build Guide for Durin (F2P)

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose Your F2P Weapon">
    Select **Moonweaver's Dawn** for maximum Burst DMG, **Amenoma Kageuchi** for Energy Recharge, or **Harbinger of Dawn** for CRIT stats (if you can maintain high HP). Consider **Iron Sting** for Elemental Mastery in reaction-focused teams.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Artifact Set Selection">
    The new set, **A Day Carved from Rising Winds (4-piece)**, is generally Durin's Best-in-Slot, especially when paired with Hexerei members. Otherwise, **Noblesse Oblige (4-piece)** is excellent for its Burst DMG bonus and team ATK buff, or **Emblem of Severed Fate (4-piece)** for ER and Burst DMG.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Artifact Main Stats">
    For the Sands, aim for **ATK%** or **Elemental Mastery** (for reaction teams). The Goblet should be **Pyro DMG Bonus**. The Circlet should be **CRIT Rate** or **CRIT DMG**, depending on your weapon and substat balance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Focus on Artifact Substats">
    Prioritize **CRIT Rate**, **CRIT DMG**, **ATK%**, and **Elemental Mastery**. If Durin is your only Pyro character, some **Energy Recharge (110-140%)** might be necessary, otherwise, his Elemental Skill at level 10 can mitigate ER needs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Talent Leveling Priority">
    Durin's primary source of damage and utility comes from his **Elemental Burst** and **Elemental Skill**. Prioritize leveling these two talents first. Normal Attack only becomes a higher priority if you intend to play him as an on-field DPS in his Dark Form, especially with C6 Bennett.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Artifact Substat Balance">
While aiming for high CRIT stats is important, don't neglect ATK% and Elemental Mastery. A balanced distribution often leads to more consistent damage, especially when factoring in Durin's A4 passive which scales with ATK.
</Callout>
