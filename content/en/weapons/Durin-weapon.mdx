It provides a high Base ATK and a substantial Crit Rate secondary stat, which is vital since Durin ascends with Crit DMG.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimization: Artifact & Weapon Synergy">

Choosing a **Durin weapon** is only half the battle; you must pair it with the correct artifact set to maximize his 20-second Burst uptime. In 2026, the **A Day Carved from Rising Winds** set is his Best-in-Slot, especially when using a weapon that provides Crit Rate.

**Key Synergies to Consider:**
- **ATK Threshold**: Durin’s A4 passive, *Chaos Formed Like the Night*, caps at **2500 ATK**. If your weapon has a low base (like Lion's Roar), you must use an ATK% Sands and potentially an ATK% Goblet.
- **Energy Recharge (ER)**: If your weapon doesn't provide ER (like Jade Cutter), and your Skill isn't crowned, aim for **130% - 150% ER** to ensure a smooth rotation.
- **Reaction Buffs**: In **Dark Form**, Durin gains a 40% (or 70% with Hexerei) Vape/Melt bonus. Weapons with Elemental Mastery secondary stats (like Xiphos' Moonlight) become significantly more valuable in these builds.
