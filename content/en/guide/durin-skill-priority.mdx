<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Form Transmutation: Purity vs. Darkness">

Durin's gameplay revolves around the **Essential Transmutation** mechanic triggered by his Elemental Skill. Unlike standard characters, his skill has a follow-up action that determines his state for the next 30 seconds. This choice completely alters the behavior of his Elemental Burst and the buffs provided by his Ascension 1 passive.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Skill Cast">
    Cast the Elemental Skill **Binary Form: Convergence and Division** to deal initial AoE Pyro damage and enter the Transmutation state.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose Your Path">
    Press the **Skill Button again** to enter the **Confirmation of Purity (White Form)** or perform a **Normal Attack** to enter the **Denial of Darkness (Dark Form)**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Energy Refund">
    Upon entering either state, Durin instantly restores flat energy. This refund occurs once every 6 seconds and scales with talent level.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unleash the Burst">
    The Elemental Burst will now take the form of the **Dragon of White Flame** or the **Dragon of Dark Decay** based on the chosen state.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Talent Scaling and Energy Management">

A critical aspect of the **durin skill priority** is the energy refund provided by his skill. At talent level 10, entering a form restores **33 flat energy**. Since his Burst cost is 70, this effectively reduces the requirement to **37 energy**. This mechanic is why high-investment players often crown the skill alongside the burst.

| Talent Level | Energy Refunded | Effective Burst Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Level 1 | 15.0 Energy | 55.0 Energy |
| Level 6 | 24.0 Energy | 46.0 Energy |
| Level 9 | 30.0 Energy | 40.0 Energy |
| **Level 10** | **33.0 Energy** | **37.0 Energy** |
| Level 13 (C5) | 42.0 Energy | 28.0 Energy |

Durin's A4 passive, **Chaos Formed Like the Night**, provides a significant damage boost based on his total ATK. For every 100 ATK, the damage of the first 10 Burst ticks increases by 3%, capping at 75% at 2500 ATK.

| ATK Stat | Burst DMG Multiplier | Usage Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1500 ATK | +45% DMG | Low Investment/Support |
| 2000 ATK | +60% DMG | Standard Sub-DPS |
| **2500 ATK** | **+75% DMG** | **Optimal Scaling Cap** |

<Callout type="success" title="Scaling Insight">
Because Durin scales so heavily with ATK up to the 2500 cap, an **ATK% Goblet** is often competitive with a Pyro DMG Goblet if you are below the threshold and lack external ATK buffs like Bennett or Noblesse Oblige.
</Callout>

</Section>
