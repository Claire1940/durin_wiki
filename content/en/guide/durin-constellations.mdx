<Section icon={Swords} title="Detailed Constellation Breakdown">

Each of Durin's six constellations serves a specific purpose, ranging from additive damage buffs to massive defense penetration. Unlike some characters who have "dead" constellations, Durin's kit scales consistently from C1 through C6.

| Level | Name | Effect Summary | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **C1** | Adamah's Redemption | Adds 20 stacks of Cycle of Enlightenment damage | **High** |
| **C2** | Unground Visions | 50% DMG Bonus to Pyro and reaction elements | **Essential** |
| **C3** | Flame Mirror's Revelation | Increases Elemental Burst level by 3 | Medium |
| **C4** | Emanare's Source | 40% Burst DMG + 30% chance to save stacks | Medium |
| **C5** | Scouring Flame's Sundering | Increases Elemental Skill level by 3 | Low |
| **C6** | Dual Birth | 30% DEF Ignore + 30% Shred or 40% extra Ignore | **Massive** |

**C1: Adamah's Redemption**
This is a "Quill" style mechanic. In White Mode, it buffs your active character's attacks by 60% of Durin's ATK for 20 hits. In Dark Mode, it buffs Durin's own Burst hits by a staggering 150% of his ATK for 10 hits. It is a significant floor raiser for team DPS.

**C2: Unground Visions**
Arguably his most powerful early constellation. Providing a **50% damage bonus** to Pyro and the secondary element in any triggered reaction (Hydro, Cryo, Electro, etc.) for the entire team. This makes him a universal buffer comparable to top-tier Anemo supports but without the need for strict VV swirling.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Note">
C2's buff lasts for 20 seconds, matching the uptime of his Burst. This ensures nearly **100% uptime** on the 50% damage bonus in standard rotations.
</Callout>
