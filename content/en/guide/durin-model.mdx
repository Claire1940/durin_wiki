### Durin's Evolution in Genshin Impact

The character's journey began as an NPC, Mini Durin, before his full human appearance was revealed. His integration into Mondstadt society and eventual playable status mark a significant development in the game's narrative.

*   **Version 4.7 (July 5, 2024)**: Evil dragon of Simulanka introduced in trailer.
*   **Version 4.8 (July 23, 2025)**: NPC Mini Durin introduced in event quest.
*   **Version 5.6 (2025)**: Mini Durin's human body revealed in Archon Quest.
*   **Version 5.7 (July 22, 2025)**: Mini Durin's full human appearance revealed, name changed to Durin.
*   **Version Luna III (2025)**: Durin released as a playable character.
