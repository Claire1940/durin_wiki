Durin is a highly flexible 5-star Pyro Sword character who excels as an off-field Sub-DPS. Each form significantly alters the behavior of his Elemental Burst, making him adaptable to almost any team archetype in the 2026 meta.

<Section icon={Target} title="Best Artifacts and Stat Priority">

Building Durin requires a balance of Attack, Crit, and occasionally Elemental Mastery. His **A4 Passive (Chaos Formed Like the Night)** is the most critical factor to consider, as it increases his Burst damage based on his total Attack stat. To maximize this buff (75% damage increase), Durin needs to reach **2500 Attack**.

<Callout type="warning" title="Energy Recharge Warning">
While Durin's Skill provides a massive energy refund, his requirements vary by team. In solo-Pyro teams without a crowned Skill, you may still need **140% - 160% Energy Recharge** to maintain 100% Burst uptime.
</Callout>

| Artifact Set | Ranking | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **A Day Carved from Rising Winds** | ★★★★★ | Best-in-slot for 2026; provides massive ATK and Crit Rate. |
| **Noblesse Oblige** | ★★★★☆ | Best for pure support roles to buff the main DPS. |
| **Crimson Witch of Flames** | ★★★★☆ | Excellent for Dark Form in Vaporize or Overvape teams. |
| **Emblem of Severed Fate** | ★★★☆☆ | Use only if you struggle significantly with Energy Recharge. |

**Main Stat Recommendations:**
- **Sands**: ATK% (preferred) or Energy Recharge (if below 120%).
- **Goblet**: Pyro DMG Bonus (ATK% Goblet is viable if using Furina).
- **Circlet**: Crit Rate or Crit DMG (maintain a 1:2 ratio).

<Callout type="tip" title="Substat Priority">
Focus on **Crit Rate/DMG > ATK% > Elemental Mastery (Dark Form) > Energy Recharge**. Since Durin scales heavily with Attack, flat ATK rolls are more valuable on him than on most other characters.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Investment and Progression">

To get the most out of your Durin, follow this prioritized progression path. Investing in his levels and talents is more impactful than hunting for perfect artifact substats early on.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Level to 90 (or 100)">
    As a Sub-DPS, Durin benefits significantly from the base stats gained at higher levels. In the 2026 update, leveling him to 100 provides a roughly 10% total damage increase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Elemental Burst">
    The majority of Durin's damage and utility comes from his Burst. Crown this first to maximize the multipliers for both forms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Level Skill to 10">
    This is essential for his energy economy. At level 10, the 33 flat energy restoration makes him incredibly easy to build, allowing you to swap ER pieces for more ATK or Crit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach C2 for Vertical Investment">
    If you are pulling for constellations, **C2** is the best stopping point. It provides a 50% damage bonus to the entire team for Pyro-related reactions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Constellation | Importance | Effect Summary |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **C1** | ★★★★☆ | Adds ATK-based 'Quills' to team attacks (White) or personal hits (Dark). |
| **C2** | ★★★★★ | **Best Value**; 50% Elemental DMG bonus for the whole party. |
| **C4** | ★★★☆☆ | 40% Burst DMG boost and a chance to save C1 stacks. |
| **C6** | ★★★★★ | Massive DEF shred (30%) and DEF ignore (70% in Dark Form). |

<Callout type="warning" title="On-Field DPS Requirement">
If you intend to use Durin as an on-field Main DPS, you **must** use him with **C6 Bennett**. Durin does not have innate Pyro infusion for his normal attacks, and Bennett's infusion is required to make his Dark Form combos viable.
</Callout>
