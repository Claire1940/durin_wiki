<Section icon={{Target}} title="How Durin's Damage Profile Works">
Durin's two forms reward different stat packages, so one of the biggest mistakes in community calcs is treating him like a single-mode carry.

| Form | Main Damage Pattern | Priority Stats | Typical Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **White Dragon** | Sustained elemental pressure and utility scaling | HP%, Energy Recharge, Elemental Mastery | Hybrid support / reaction enabler |
| **Dark Dragon** | Burst-heavy personal damage | CRIT, ATK%, Damage Bonus | Main DPS / burst finisher |

<Callout type="warning" title="Do Not Mix Priorities Blindly">
A build that looks efficient in White Dragon form can underperform badly in Dark Dragon form if too much budget is spent on HP or recharge instead of direct offensive stats.
</Callout>
</Section>

<Section icon={{Swords}} title="Sample Durin Calcs by Form">
### White Dragon Example
For White Dragon teams, the practical goal is usually **stable uptime plus reaction value**, not the single biggest screenshot hit.

- **Target focus:** HP scaling, team amplification, consistent burst access
- **Best use case:** Long rotations, reaction teams, safer Abyss sides

### Dark Dragon Example
Dark Dragon calculations favor short windows with stacked buffs.

- **Target focus:** CRIT ratio, ATK scaling, front-loaded burst value
- **Best use case:** Boss phases, speed clears, shield-breaking follow-up damage
