<Section icon={Swords} title="The Abyssal Dragon: Durin's Original Form">
Durin, a name now associated with a playable Pyro character, originally struck fear into the hearts of Teyvat's inhabitants. This ancient entity was a colossal, disfigured black skeletal dragon with glowing red eyes, imbued with the corrupting power of the Abyss. Its wings were veined, and its sheer size rivaled that of Dragonspine itself, where its remains now lie.

Created by the infamous alchemist "Gold" Rhinedottir, Durin was born from the abyssal depths and classified as a "Humus" creation. Despite its destructive nature, Durin possessed a gentle heart, wishing to befriend the people of Mondstadt and Dvalin. However, its Abyssal blood unintentionally caused devastation, leading to its tragic downfall.

<Section icon={Target} title="Creation and Cataclysm: The Scourge of Mondstadt">
Five hundred years ago, during a cataclysmic event, Durin emerged as a terrifying force in Mondstadt. Rhinedottir, its creator, considered it one of her "miraculous creations," yet it lacked the "will" to control its immense, abyssal strength. This inherent flaw sealed Durin's fate from the beginning, leading to widespread destruction.

The Knights of Favonius were severely weakened after a campaign in Khaenri'ah, leaving Mondstadt vulnerable. Barbatos, the Anemo Archon, awoke to the pleas of his people and, with Dvalin, confronted Durin. Their epic battle culminated in Dvalin delivering a fatal blow to Durin's neck, sending the dragon plummeting onto the mountain now known as Dragonspine.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="The Evolution of Durin: From Dragon to Human">
The story of Durin doesn't end with its demise. Through complex alchemical processes and a desire for a different fate, a new form of Durin emerged. In the storybook world of Simulanka, a new Durin was created by Hexenzirkel witch Andersdotter, mirroring the original's gentle intentions but tragic circumstances.

Eventually, Albedo, another creation of Rhinedottir, orchestrated a miraculous transformation. By fusing the goodness of "Mini Durin" (the Simulanka counterpart) with the original Durin's core, and utilizing materials like the "flesh of an immortal monster" and Rhinedottir's "embryo," Albedo granted Durin a synthetic human body. This new, playable Pyro character now aims to integrate into Mondstadt society and create positive memories, striving to redefine the name "Durin."
