### Step-by-Step Cooking Guide for Apple Stew (Trial Version)

To cook **Durin special dish**, follow these steps carefully:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Northern Apple Stew Recipe">
    The base recipe required is the **Northern Apple Stew**. You can obtain this recipe by reaching **Reputation Level 1 in Mondstadt**. Visit Hertha, the Reputation NPC in Mondstadt, to claim your reward once you meet the requirement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Ingredients">
    Once you have the recipe, you'll need the following ingredients for **Northern Apple Stew**:
    - **Raw Meat x3**
    - **Apple x3**
    - **Butter x1**
    - **Pepper x1**
    These ingredients are commonly found throughout Teyvat. Raw Meat can be obtained from wild animals, Apples from trees, Butter can be processed at a cooking pot, and Pepper is sold by various general goods vendors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cook with Durin">
    Head to any cooking pot in Teyvat. Select the **Northern Apple Stew** recipe. Crucially, you must select **Durin** as the character to cook the dish. There's a chance that instead of the standard Northern Apple Stew, Durin will prepare his special version, the **Apple Stew (Trial Version)**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Cooking Proficiency and RNG">
While cooking with Durin gives you a chance to get his special dish, it's not guaranteed every time. The probability increases with Durin's cooking proficiency level for that specific dish. Keep cooking the Northern Apple Stew with him to maximize your chances!
</Callout>

### Alternative Acquisition: Durin's Birthday

Another way to obtain the **Durin special dish** is through the in-game mail. On **March 14, 2026**, which is Durin's birthday, players receive a special mail containing one **Apple Stew (Trial Version)**. This is a guaranteed way to get the dish, albeit only once a year.
