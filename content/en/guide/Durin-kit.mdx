### Understanding Essential Transmutation

To master Durin, you must understand the two primary states triggered by his Elemental Skill. The state you choose determines the behavior of the "Dragon" manifested during his Burst.

| State | Activation Combo | Burst Behavior | Primary Role |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Confirmation of Purity** | Skill > Skill | AoE Pyro damage every 1s (20s duration) | Support / Sub-DPS |
| **Denial of Darkness** | Skill > Normal Attack | Single-target high scaling every 1.25s | Reaction Carry |

<Callout type="info" title="Rotation Tip">
If you enter combat with full energy, Durin defaults to **White Mode**. If you need Dark Mode immediately, you must perform the Skill > NA combo before bursting. Note that his energy refund (30-33 flat energy) triggers upon entering either state, effectively reducing his 70-cost Burst to a manageable 37-40 cost.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Passive Talents and Scaling Thresholds">

Durin's damage and utility are heavily frontloaded into his Ascension Passives. These passives are what make him a "must-have" for specific niche teams, such as Mono-Geo or Anemo Carry comps, due to his rare ability to shred non-standard resistances.

### A1 Passive: Light Manifest of the Divine Calculus
This passive provides different buffs based on his active mode. In **White Mode**, triggering Pyro-related reactions (Overload, Swirl, Crystallize, Burning) reduces the target's Pyro RES and the corresponding element's RES by 20%. In **Dark Mode**, Durin personally gains 40% Vaporize and Melt damage.

### A4 Passive: Chaos Formed Like the Night
This is the most critical scaling factor in the **Durin kit**. After bursting, Durin gains 10 stacks of **Primordial Fusion**. Each stack increases his Burst damage by 3% for every 100 ATK he possesses. To maximize this, players should aim for a specific "effective ATK" target.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Artifact Selection and Stat Priorities">

Building Durin requires a balance between his ATK scaling and typical Crit stats. Because he gains massive flat energy from his skill, his Energy Recharge (ER) requirements are surprisingly low compared to other 70-cost Burst units.

### Best Artifact Sets

| Set Name | Recommendation | Best Playstyle |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **A Day Carved from Rising Winds** | Best-in-Slot (BiS) | General / Hexerei Teams |
| **Noblesse Oblige** | Excellent | Support / Sub-DPS |
| **Crimson Witch of Flames** | Strong | Dark Mode (Vape/Melt) |
| **Marechaussee Hunter** | Situational | On-field with Furina |
| **Emblem of Severed Fate** | Decent | High ER requirements only |

### Recommended Main Stats

- **Sands**: ATK% (Standard) or Elemental Mastery (Reaction-heavy Dark Mode)
- **Goblet**: Pyro DMG Bonus or ATK% (Choose the one with better sub-stats)
- **Circlet**: Crit Rate or Crit DMG (Maintain a 1:2 ratio)

<Callout type="warning" title="Energy Recharge Note">
If Durin's Elemental Skill is **Crowned (Level 10)**, his ER needs drop to almost zero in double-Pyro or Overload teams. In solo-Pyro teams, aim for **130% - 150% ER** to ensure 100% Burst uptime.
</Callout>

### Free-to-Play Options
For players looking for accessible options, the **Finale of the Deep** (Fontaine Craftable) provides a huge ATK boost if you have a healer to clear the Bond of Life. Alternatively, **Moonweaver's Dawn** is a reward from the "Echoes of an Unfinished Past" quest and serves as an excellent generalist sword.
