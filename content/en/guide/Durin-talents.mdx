<Section icon={Target} title="Durin Talents: Skill and Burst Scaling">

To maximize the impact of **Durin talents**, players must prioritize his Elemental Burst, **Principle of Purity: As the Light Shifts**. This talent manifests a dragon that follows the active character, ensuring that your Pyro application is never tethered to a static location like Bennett's circle or Jean's field.

### Elemental Burst: The Dragon Forms
The Burst duration lasts for **20 seconds** with an 18-second cooldown. This provides over 100% potential uptime, a rarity for high-impact sub-DPS units.

| Feature | Dragon of White Flame (Light) | Dragon of Dark Decay (Dark) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **DMG Type** | Area of Effect (AoE) | Single-Target |
| **Attack Rate** | Every 1.0 seconds | Every 1.25 seconds |
| **ICD Pattern** | Pyro every 2nd hit | Standard ICD |
| **Special Effect** | Res Shred (via A1) | Personal Reaction Buff (via A1) |

### Passive Talents: The 2,500 ATK Threshold
His A4 passive, **Chaos Formed Like the Night**, provides a unique scaling mechanic. For every 100 ATK Durin possesses, his burst damage increases by 3% of the original damage, capping at a **75% total increase** when he reaches 2,500 ATK.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Stat Note">
Durin's burst **does not snapshot**. This means if you step out of a buffing field (like Bennett's) or a temporary artifact buff expires, his dragon's damage will drop immediately. Always ensure your buffs are active throughout his 20-second window.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimal Build: Artifacts and Weapons">

Building Durin requires a balance of Attack, Crit, and occasionally Elemental Mastery. Because his skill provides a massive energy refund (30-33 energy), his **Energy Recharge (ER)** needs are surprisingly low.

### Artifact Set Rankings
1.  **Noblesse Oblige (4pc)**: The standard support/sub-DPS choice. Since his burst lasts 20s, the 12s attack buff won't have 100% uptime, but the personal burst damage is invaluable.
2.  **Emblem of Severed Fate (4pc)**: Viable if you have high-quality pieces, though Durin doesn't need the ER as much as other units.
3.  **Maréchaussée Hunter (4pc)**: The **Best-in-Slot for On-Field Durin** when paired with Furina.
4.  **2pc Mix (Crimson Witch / ATK%)**: A highly efficient alternative if you lack a full set with good substats.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Constellation Guide: Investment Milestones">

While Durin is exceptionally strong at C0, his constellations provide significant vertical scaling for both his support and carry roles.

  <Step num="2" title="C2: Unground Visions">
