Durin, the newest Pyro five-star sword user in Genshin Impact, offers a versatile playstyle as either a Sub-DPS or support. Understanding his base stats and how they scale with ascension is crucial for optimizing his performance. Durin's ascension bonus stat is Crit DMG%, making him naturally inclined towards high critical damage output as he levels up.

**Video Highlights:**
- Durin is a versatile Pyro character with two modes: White (support/Sub-DPS) and Dark (personal damage/carry).
- His Elemental Skill generates particles and refunds energy, reducing his ER needs.
- White mode offers RES shred, while Dark mode boosts Vaporize/Melt damage.
- He has great synergy with Hexerei units, increasing passive effects by 75%.
- His A4 passive increases Burst damage based on ATK, up to 2500 effective ATK.

<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal Stat Prioritization for Durin">
When building Durin, understanding which stats to prioritize on artifacts is essential for maximizing his damage and utility in various team compositions.

### Main Stat Priority

| Artifact Slot | Primary Stat | Alternative Stat (Situational) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sands of Eon** | ATK% | Energy Recharge (if solo Pyro) / Elemental Mastery (Burgeon/Vape/Melt) |
| **Goblet of Eonothem** | Pyro DMG Bonus | ATK% (if low on ATK buffs) |
| **Circlet of Logos** | CRIT Rate% | CRIT DMG% (if CRIT Rate is already high) |

<Callout type="info">
Durin's Elemental Skill, when leveled up to 9 or 10, provides a substantial energy refund (30-33 energy). This often means he needs very little Energy Recharge, especially in teams with other Pyro characters or strong particle generation.
</Callout>

### Sub-Stat Priority

1.  **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG**: Always aim for a balanced ratio, generally 1:2.
2.  **ATK%**: Highly valuable, especially up to ~2500 effective ATK to maximize his A4 passive.
3.  **Elemental Mastery**: Crucial for reaction-based teams (Vaporize, Melt, Burgeon, Overload).
4.  **Energy Recharge**: Build only as needed to consistently cast his Elemental Burst.
