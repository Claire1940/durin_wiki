<Section icon={Swords} title="The Tragic Origins of Durin and Dvalin's Conflict">

The saga of **Durin and Dvalin** is one of the most poignant tales in Genshin Impact's lore, rooted in misunderstanding and unfortunate circumstances. Dvalin, the noble Anemo Dragon and one of the Four Winds, was a beloved protector of Mondstadt, deeply connected to Barbatos (Venti). His existence was one of harmony and defense, guarding the land from threats.

Durin, on the other hand, was a creation of the alchemist Rhinedottir, also known as "Gold," through the Art of Khemia. He was an innocent being, described as a "baby dragon" at the time, who merely wished to experience the world and form connections. However, his very essence, his blood, was poisonous to the flourishing lands of Mondstadt, leading to widespread fear and destruction wherever he went.

The people of Mondstadt, unaware of Durin's true intentions, perceived him as a monstrous threat. This led to a devastating battle where Dvalin, alongside Barbatos, confronted Durin. In a fierce struggle, Dvalin ultimately defeated Durin, tearing out his throat and heart, causing the poisoned dragon to fall upon what would become Dragonspine.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Dvalin's Burden and Recovery">

Dvalin's victory over Durin came at a heavy cost. During the battle, he was exposed to Durin's venomous ichor, which seeped into his body. This poison caused immense pain and suffering, forcing Dvalin into a deep slumber within an ancient ruin. For centuries, he lay dormant, his pain eventually being manipulated by the Abyss Order.

The Abyss Order exploited Dvalin's suffering and his long separation from Barbatos, whispering lies and fostering resentment. This manipulation turned Dvalin, once a noble protector, into "Stormterror," a source of terror and destruction for Mondstadt. He attacked the city he once guarded, fueled by his pain and the Abyss Order's deceit.

<Callout type="info" title="The Abyss Order's Influence">
The Abyss Order played a crucial role in exacerbating Dvalin's suffering and turning him against Mondstadt, showcasing their insidious nature.
</Callout>
