<Section icon={Target} title="Act I: A Mother's Last Wish">
The initial act of the **Durin quest** sets the stage for a profound adventure. The Traveler and Paimon are telepathically contacted by Alice, who directs them to Starsand Shoal to meet Durin. Here, they encounter Durin in a moment of vulnerability, struggling with his Abyssal energies. Alice presents a book—a combined work of the Hexenzirkel witches—tasking Durin with resolving its unfinished narrative, a dying wish of Andersdotter.

<Callout type="warning">
Be prepared for emotional moments as Durin grapples with his identity and past. The quest delves deep into his origins and the intentions of his creator.
</Callout>

The journey begins in Mondstadt, specifically its library, where they seek out Lisa. She provides insight into Andersdotter's hidden books, which mysteriously disappear and reappear. Upon retrieving these books, the Traveler, Paimon, and Durin are drawn into the storybook itself, where they witness the hatching of a baby Durin, hinting at the story's true subject.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Acts II & III: Prophecy and Fate's Wiles">
The **Durin quest** continues with "A Prophecy From the Past," where Venti plays a crucial role. Durin and the Traveler seek Venti's guidance regarding the unfinished story. Venti directs them to a mages' meeting spot, where playing a melody transports them back into the storybook. Here, they encounter four Dodoco-shaped witches celebrating baby Durin's arrival, prompting the trio to choose a gift for him. This segment explores themes of destiny and the unknown future.

<Callout type="success">
Engage with Venti's unique perspective on storytelling and fate. His insights provide valuable context to Andersdotter's motivations.
</Callout>

The subsequent act, "Fate's Accursed Wiles," takes a dramatic turn. In Mondstadt, Durin experiences a sudden burst of Abyssal energy, causing him to collapse. Marjorie and Shuyun's suspicion highlights the world's lingering fear of the original Durin. Jean steps in, taking Durin into custody and entrusting the book to Albedo for examination, concerned about the potential harm if Durin's powers go unchecked.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Acts IV & V: Revelation and Solution">
"Revelation Beneath the Stars" sees Durin recovering and the group heading to Sumeru City. They encounter the Wanderer, who reveals that any attempt to write about Durin is inexplicably altered to reflect the historical, evil Durin. As a Descender, the Wanderer understands that Irminsul's changes mean the world struggles to accept this new, separate Durin. He joins the quest, and together they venture into a simulated Sumeru within the book, discovering the Hexenzirkel's ingenious plan for Durin's existence.

<Callout type="info">
The Wanderer's unique status as a Descender provides crucial insight into the mechanics of Irminsul and its impact on collective memory, directly affecting the perception of Durin.
</Callout>

The final act, "The Solution of Fate," brings them to Dragonspine to meet Albedo. He explains the Hexenzirkel's grand plan, involving Barbeloth's astrology, Rhinedottir's alchemy, and Alice's magic, to allow Durin to live as a separate entity. Within the storybook, they confront a symbolic heart, leading to a "bad ending" that Alice's magic reverses. Ultimately, Alice, with Venti's help, facilitates Durin's final regret: befriending Dvalin. Andersdotter's consciousness bids farewell, and Alice gifts them a copy of the newly completed book, signifying a triumphant conclusion to the **Durin quest**.
