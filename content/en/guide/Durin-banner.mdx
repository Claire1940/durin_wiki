The **Durin banner** not only brings the 5-star Pyro Sword DPS Durin but also features a selection of powerful 4-star characters and unique weapons. Understanding the entire banner's contents is essential for making informed wishing decisions.

### 5-Star Character: Durin
Durin is a 5-star Pyro Sword DPS with a versatile kit. He can operate in two states: "Principle of Purity: As the Light Shifts" and "Principle of Darkness: As the Stars Smolder."

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Principle of Purity">
    - Deals **AoE Pyro DMG**.
    - Summons **Dragon of White Flame** for continuous AoE Pyro DMG.
    - Decreases Pyro RES and corresponding Element RES of opponents after certain reactions (Burning, Overloaded, Pyro Swirl, Pyro Crystallize).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Principle of Darkness">
    - Deals **AoE Pyro DMG**.
    - Summons **Dragon of Dark Decay** for single-target Pyro DMG.
    - Increases **Vaporize DMG by 40%** and **Melt DMG by 40%**.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
