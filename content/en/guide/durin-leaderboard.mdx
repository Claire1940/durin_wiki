<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate Denial Form">
    To enter Denial form, use Durin's Elemental Skill, followed by one Normal Attack, and then activate his Elemental Burst. This form is excellent for consistent off-field Pyro application and reaction triggers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Purity Form">
    For Purity form, activate Durin's Elemental Skill twice, then use his Elemental Burst. This form offers a faster Burst strike rate and wider AoE Pyro application, ideal for certain team compositions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maximize Reaction Windows">
    Time your other characters' elemental applications to coincide with Durin's Burst hits, especially for Vaporize and Melt reactions, to maximize damage output.
  </Step>
</Steps>
