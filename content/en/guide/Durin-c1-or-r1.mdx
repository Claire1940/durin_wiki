<Section icon={Trophy} title="Understanding Durin's C1: Adamah's Redemption">
Durin's C1, "Adamah's Redemption," provides a substantial boost to his Elemental Burst, with effects varying based on his current form (Purity or Darkness). This Constellation enhances both his personal damage and his team support capabilities.

In his Purity state (White Form), C1 grants 20 stacks of Cycle of Enlightenment (CoE) to all other nearby party members for 20 seconds. Each stack consumed by an active character's attacks (Normal, Charged, Plunging, Skill, Burst) increases their damage by 60% of Durin's ATK. This acts as an Additive Base DMG Bonus, similar to Shenhe's Icy Quill or Yun Jin's Burst buff, significantly empowering your main DPS.

When in his Darkness state (Dark Form), C1 shifts its focus to Durin's personal damage. He gains 20 stacks of CoE for 20 seconds, and each instance of his Elemental Burst damage consumes 2 stacks to increase its damage by 150% of Durin's ATK. This means 10 of his Burst hits will receive a substantial buff.

<Callout type="success" title="C1 Benefits">
Durin's C1 provides excellent **flexibility**, enhancing either **team damage** or his **personal Burst damage** based on his form. This makes him a more versatile unit in various team archetypes.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Durin's R1 Signature Weapon: Athame Artis">
Athame Artis is Durin's Best-in-Slot 5-star Sword, designed to perfectly complement his kit. At R1 (Refinement Rank 1), it offers strong base stats and a powerful passive that enhances his damage and provides team buffs.

At Level 90, Athame Artis boasts a **608 Base ATK** and a **33.1% CRIT Rate** secondary stat. Its passive effect increases CRIT DMG from Elemental Bursts by 16%. Additionally, when an Elemental Burst hits an opponent, it grants the "Blade of the Daylight Hours" effect: Durin's ATK is increased by 20%, and nearby active party members receive a 16% ATK increase for 3 seconds. This ATK bonus is further amplified by an additional 75% if the Hexerei: Secret Rite effect is active, making it even more potent with Hexerei teammates. This effect can trigger even when Durin is off-field.

<Callout type="warning" title="Weapon Dependency">
While powerful, Athame Artis's full potential is realized with the Hexerei: Secret Rite effect active. This means you'll ideally want another Hexerei character in your party to maximize its benefits.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="C1 vs. R1: Which is the Better Investment?">
The choice between Durin's C1 and R1 Athame Artis often comes down to your current roster, available resources, and specific team goals.

For most players, especially those looking to maximize overall team damage, **Durin's C1 is generally considered a superior investment compared to his R1 signature weapon.** The KQM guide suggests that C1 typically results in a higher team DPS increase than R1, assuming a baseline of C0 Durin with an R1 Wolf-Fang.

### Decision Factors
