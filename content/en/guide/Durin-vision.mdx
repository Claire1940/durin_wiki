<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Talents and Elemental Mechanics">

The true power of the **Durin vision** lies in his Elemental Skill, **Binary Form: Convergence and Division**. This talent allows Durin to enter two distinct states: the **Denial of Darkness** and the **Confirmation of Purity**. These states alter his Normal Attacks and provide varied buffs to the team, making him a versatile pick for different combat scenarios.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Activate Binary Form">
    Cast the Elemental Skill to enter the preparation state, replacing standard attacks with special alternatives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose Your State">
    Tap Normal Attack for **Denial of Darkness** (High Pyro DMG) or the Skill button for **Confirmation of Purity** (Supportive buffs).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unleash Elemental Burst">
    Cast his Burst to trigger **Cycle of Enlightenment** stacks, which significantly boost party DMG based on Durin's ATK.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Skill Mechanic">
In the **Denial of Darkness** state, Durin's attacks are infused with Pyro and deal increased AoE damage, perfect for clearing waves of enemies.
</Callout>
