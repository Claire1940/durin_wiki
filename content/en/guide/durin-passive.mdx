The core of any build relies on mastering the **durin passive** known as *Binary Form: Convergence and Division* (A1). This passive forces a choice between team utility and raw personal power. Unlike traditional Pyro units, Durin does not have a static role; his passive adapts based on the input used immediately following his Elemental Skill.

The A1 passive functions as a toggle. If you trigger a Pyro-related reaction (Overload, Swirl, Burning, or Crystallize) while in Purity Mode, enemies lose 20% RES to both Pyro and the reacting element. In Darkness Mode, Durin pivots to a selfish carry, boosting his own Vaporize and Melt damage by 40%.

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Insight">
The RES shred in Purity Mode does **not** apply to Vaporize or Melt. If you are running a Hydro or Cryo support, you must utilize Darkness Mode to see any benefit from his A1 passive.
</Callout>

| **Purity** | Skill > Skill | **20% RES Shred** (Pyro + Element) | Overload, Burning, Mono-Pyro |
| **Darkness** | Skill > Normal Attack | **40% Vape/Melt DMG** | Vaporize, Forward Melt |
| **Default** | Burst (no skill) | **Purity Mode** (Orange) | Quick-swap Support |

<Section icon={Target} title="Primordial Fusion: The A4 ATK Scaling Cap">

While the A1 passive defines his role, the **durin passive** unlocked at Ascension 4, *Chaos Formed Like the Night*, defines his gear requirements. This passive grants Durin 10 stacks of "Primordial Fusion" upon casting his Burst. Each stack increases the damage of a Burst hit by 3% for every 100 ATK Durin possesses.

To maximize this effect, players aim for a specific "sweet spot" in stats. Because the maximum damage increase is capped at 75%, any ATK beyond a certain point yields diminishing returns compared to Crit stats.

| Total ATK | DMG Increase per Hit | Percentage of Max Buff |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1000 ATK** | 30% | 40% |
| **1500 ATK** | 45% | 60% |
| **2000 ATK** | 60% | 80% |
| **2500 ATK** | **75% (MAX)** | **100%** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Stat Balancing">
Do not sacrifice a Crit DMG circlet just to hit 2500 ATK. The A4 passive increases your **multiplier (motion value)**, meaning Crit stats still act as a final multiplier on top of this buff. Use Bennett or Noblesse Oblige to bridge the ATK gap.
</Callout>

The A4 stacks are consumed one by one as the "Dragon of White Flame" or "Dragon of Dark Decay" strikes. If a single attack hits multiple enemies, it still only consumes one stack, making Durin exceptionally potent in AoE scenarios where his Purity Mode shines.

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Operational Guide: Activating the Correct Passive">

Activating the specific **durin passive** you need requires muscle memory. Because his Burst defaults to Purity Mode if cast without a prior Skill, many players accidentally lose out on the Darkness Mode buffs in Vaporize teams.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Skill Cast">
    Press E (Elemental Skill). Durin will perform a quick dash and enter a transitional state for 5 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mode Selection">
    - For **Purity**: Press E again.
    - For **Darkness**: Perform one Normal Attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Burst Execution">
    Cast Q (Elemental Burst). The animation will be **Orange** for Purity or **Dark Red** for Darkness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Passive Maintenance">
    The chosen mode lasts for 30 seconds. You can swap Durin off-field, and the Dragon will continue to benefit from the selected passive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Pro Rotation">
In a standard rotation, use Durin's Skill twice (Purity) to generate particles first, then swap to your main DPS. The 35% RES shred (with resonance) will persist even if Durin is off-field, significantly boosting your team's total output.
</Callout>
