<Section icon={Map} title="The Genesis of Durin and the Hexenzirkel's Influence">

The story of Durin, the great dragon, is deeply interwoven with the enigmatic Hexenzirkel, a coven of powerful witches in the world of Teyvat. While the historical Durin was a creature of destruction, the Hexenzirkel, particularly the witch Andersdotter, sought to rewrite his fate within the storybook world of Simulanka. This ambitious undertaking aimed to give Durin a happier ending, a stark contrast to his real-world tragic demise.

The Hexenzirkel's involvement extended beyond mere storytelling. Through the intricate Art of Khemia, Albedo, a creation of the Hexenzirkel witch Rhinedottir, was instrumental in granting Durin a synthetic human body. This transformation allowed Durin to integrate into Mondstadt society, a testament to the Hexenzirkel's profound and often unpredictable influence on Teyvat's history and its inhabitants.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Artifact and Weapon Recommendations for Durin">

Choosing the right artifacts and weapons is paramount for Durin to shine in combat. His ATK scaling and Pyro element open up several viable options for both Free-to-Play (F2P) and whale players.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Recommended Artifact Sets">
    - **Crimson Witch of Flames (4-piece):** Ideal for Vaporize/Melt teams, significantly boosts Pyro DMG and reaction damage.
    - **Emblem of Severed Fate (4-piece):** Great for Elemental Burst-focused builds, especially if Energy Recharge is a concern.
    - **Noblesse Oblige (4-piece):** Provides a team-wide ATK buff after using his Elemental Burst, suitable for support-oriented builds.
    - **Shimenawa's Reminiscence (4-piece):** Boosts Normal/Charged Attack damage, but consumes Energy, requiring careful management.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recommended Main Stats">
    - **Sands:** ATK% or Energy Recharge% (if needed)
    - **Goblet:** Pyro DMG Bonus%
    - **Circlet:** CRIT Rate% or CRIT DMG% (balance for 1:2 ratio)
    - **Substats Priority:** CRIT Rate/DMG > ATK% > Energy Recharge > Elemental Mastery
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
