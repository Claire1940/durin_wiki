The Super-Heavy Landrover: Mechanized Fortress possesses a range of attacks that players must learn to counter. These include wide-area attacks, rapid melee strikes, and elemental-infused abilities.

| Attack Name | Description | Recommended Counter |

| **Ground Stomp** | The boss stomps, creating an AoE blast. | Use i-frames from bursts or dashes, or move out of the AoE quickly. |
| **Cryo Ball Barrage** | Fires multiple Cryo projectiles, leaving ice patches. | Continuously move to avoid projectiles and steer clear of ice zones. |
| **Spinning Attack** | The boss spins rapidly, dealing continuous damage. | Maintain distance or use a shield character. |
