<Section icon={Swords} title="Durin's Kit Overview & Playstyle">
Durin is a versatile Pyro swordsman who primarily functions as a support, offering off-field Pyro application and significant damage amplification. His kit revolves around two distinct modes accessible via his Elemental Skill.

<Callout type="success" title="Key Abilities">
- **Elemental Skill (E)**: Switches between Support and DPS modes, provides significant energy generation (33 energy at max level).
- **Elemental Burst (Q)**: Deals damage and activates his A1 passive based on the chosen mode.
- **A1 Passive (Support Mode)**: Reduces target's elemental resistance by 20% for elements involved in Pyro reactions (excluding Vaporize/Melt). This is boosted to 35% in Hexade teams.
- **A1 Passive (DPS Mode)**: Grants Durin's own Vaporize/Melt damage a 40% buff, increased to 70% in Hexade teams.
</Callout>

**Skill Usage:**

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Energy Management">
    Durin's skill provides substantial energy. If his Burst is ready, prioritize using his Burst (Q) then Skill (E) to avoid energy overflow. If not, use Skill (E) first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mode Selection">
    Using his skill twice (EE) activates Support Mode, while using it once (E) followed by normal attacks activates DPS Mode. Choose based on your team's needs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="A4 Passive (Talent Multiplier Buff)">
    His A4 passive buffs his Burst damage by up to 1.75x based on his ATK, with 10 charges that consume only one stack per AoE hit. Aim for at least 2.5k ATK for maximum benefit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Durin excels in enabling unique team compositions by providing crucial resistance shred or personal damage buffs.
</Section>
