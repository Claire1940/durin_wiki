### MBTI: The Idealistic INFP
Durin is often typed as an **INFP** (Introverted, Intuitive, Feeling, Perceiving). This type is characterized by:
-   **Introverted Feeling (Fi)**: His primary cognitive function, meaning he prioritizes internal values, authenticity, and a strong sense of self. This explains his pursuit of a unique identity beyond his origins.
-   **Extraverted Intuition (Ne)**: His auxiliary function, leading him to explore possibilities, connect abstract ideas, and be open to new experiences. This aligns with his eagerness to learn about the human world and form new relationships.

### Enneagram: The Individualistic 4w5
Durin's Enneagram type is identified as **4w5**, the Individualist with a wing of Investigator.
-   **Type 4 (The Individualist)**: Driven by the need to be unique and significant, often feeling a sense of longing or being different from others. This resonates with his origin as a crafted being trying to forge his own path.
-   **Wing 5 (The Investigator)**: Adds a layer of intellectual curiosity and a desire for knowledge and understanding, reinforcing his efforts to study and comprehend human existence.
