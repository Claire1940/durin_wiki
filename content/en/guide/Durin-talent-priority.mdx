<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Durin's Core Kit">
Durin, a 5-star Pyro Sword user, is a versatile character capable of acting as an off-field Sub-DPS, support, or even an on-field Main DPS. His kit revolves around two distinct modes: White Mode (Purity) and Dark Mode (Darkness), which dictate his primary function in a team. Understanding how each talent contributes to these modes is crucial for optimizing your **Durin talent priority**.

His Elemental Burst is the cornerstone of his damage, providing consistent off-field Pyro application and damage. The Elemental Skill is vital for entering these modes and providing significant energy regeneration, reducing his Energy Recharge requirements. Normal Attacks are generally less important unless you commit to an on-field DPS playstyle.

**Video Highlights:**
- Durin is a versatile Pyro 5-star character with two distinct modes.

- White Mode provides AoE Pyro damage and resistance shred (except Vaporize/Melt).
- Dark Mode offers single-target Pyro damage and increased Vaporize/Melt reaction damage for Durin.
- His Burst has a high uptime (20s duration, 18s cooldown), making talent investment highly efficient.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Optimal Durin Talent Priority">
To get the most out of Durin, understanding which talents to prioritize is essential. His damage profile and utility are heavily weighted towards specific abilities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Elemental Burst (Q) - Max First">
    Durin's Elemental Burst is the primary source of his off-field damage and the core of his kit. Leveling it up first will provide the most significant increase to his overall damage output, regardless of whether you're using him in White or Dark Mode. It also dictates the strength of his continuous Pyro application.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Elemental Skill (E) - Second Priority">
    While his Elemental Skill deals some damage, its main importance lies in its ability to generate energy and allow Durin to switch between his White and Dark modes. Leveling the skill increases the amount of energy refunded to Durin (30 energy at Talent Level 9, 33 at Talent Level 10), which significantly reduces his Energy Recharge requirements and ensures consistent Burst uptime.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Normal Attack (NA) - Optional/Low Priority">
    Generally, Durin's Normal Attacks can be left unleveled if you primarily use him as an off-field support or Sub-DPS. However, if you plan to play Durin as an on-field Main DPS, especially with C6 Bennett providing Pyro infusion, then leveling his Normal Attacks becomes important. This is a more niche playstyle but can be effective in specific team compositions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Talent Leveling Caution">
Avoid over-investing in Normal Attacks unless you are committed to an on-field DPS Durin build, typically with **C6 Bennett**. For most off-field roles, the return on investment for Normal Attack talent levels is minimal compared to his Burst and Skill.
</Callout>
