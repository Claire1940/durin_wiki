<Section icon={Target} title="Durin's Constellations and Power Spikes">
Durin's constellations significantly enhance his damage output and support capabilities, offering key power spikes at different levels.

<Steps>
  <Step num="C1" title="Adamah's Redemption">
    Provides 20 instances of increased DMG for active characters (Light Form) or increased Elemental Burst DMG for Durin (Dark Form) based on his ATK. This constellation offers a substantial boost to both his personal damage and team support.
  </Step>
  <Step num="C2" title="Unground Visions">
    Amplifies Durin's DMG Bonus passive by an additional 50% for 6 seconds after triggering Pyro-related reactions or dealing Pyro/Dendro DMG to Burning opponents. This is a crucial constellation for reaction-based teams.
  </Step>
  <Step num="C6" title="Dual Birth">
    Durin's Elemental Burst DMG ignores 30% of opponents' DEF. Additionally, his Light Form Burst decreases opponent DEF by 30% for 6s, and his Dark Form Burst ignores an additional 40% DEF. This constellation provides massive damage amplification.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Constellation | Rating | Effect Highlight |
| :------------ | :----- | :--------------- |
| **C1: Adamah's Redemption** | ★★★ | Grants significant DMG increases to active characters or Durin's Burst. |
| **C2: Unground Visions** | ★★☆ | Boosts Pyro reaction damage and related elemental damage by 50%. |
| **C4: Emanare's Source** | ★★☆ | Increases Elemental Burst DMG by 40% and has a chance to save C1 stacks. |
| **C6: Dual Birth** | ★★★★★ | Provides substantial DEF ignore and DEF reduction, a huge damage increase. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Recommended Constellation Stop">
C2 is a particularly strong stopping point for many players, as it provides a significant DMG bonus to any elements involved in Pyro reactions, greatly enhancing **Durin's** utility in elemental reaction teams.
