<Section icon={Target} title="Core Mechanics: Binary Form Mastery">

Understanding the **Binary Form: Convergence and Division** is the key to playing Durin effectively. Unlike traditional stance-switchers, Durin's form is determined by his input sequence immediately following his Elemental Skill (E).

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Light Form (Confirmation of Purity)">
    - **Trigger**: Skill (E) -> Skill (E)
    - **Focus**: Team Buffing & RES Shred
    - **Effect**: 35% Elemental RES shred in Hexerei teams. Best for Overload and Burning.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dark Form (Denial of Darkness)">
    - **Trigger**: Skill (E) -> Normal Attack (NA)
    - **Focus**: Personal Reaction Damage
    - **Effect**: Massive multipliers for Vaporize/Melt. Best for On-field DPS roles.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stance Management">
Switching forms at the wrong time can disrupt your rotation. In Dark Form, his application is slightly slower (1.25s), which is optimized for triggering big reactions rather than just applying aura.
</Callout>

### Talent Priority
1. **Elemental Burst**: The primary source of both utility and damage.
2. **Elemental Skill**: Increases Energy regeneration per level; essential for rotation smoothness.
3. **Normal Attack**: Only prioritize if playing C6 On-field Dark Durin.

</Section>
