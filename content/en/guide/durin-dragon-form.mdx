<Section icon={Map} title="The Origins of Durin's Dragon Form">
Durin, a colossal dragon, holds a significant yet tragic place in Genshin Impact's lore. Created by the alchemist Rhinedottir, also known as "Gold," Durin was brought into Teyvat with an initial desire for friendship and joy. However, his very essence, infused with abyssal power, led to an unwitting path of destruction.

**Video Highlights:**
- Durin's story begins in patch 1.2 with the Chalk Prince and Dragon event.
- He was created by Rhinedottir and caused havoc in Mondstadt during the Cataclysm.
- Venti and Dvalin ultimately defeated Durin in the snowy mountains.
- Durin's blood is highly corruptive and contains abyssal power.
- Despite his actions, Durin harbored no ill will towards Dvalin or the people of Mondstadt.

<Section icon={Swords} title="The Battle and Its Aftermath">
The clash between Durin's dragon form and the protectors of Mondstadt, Venti and Dvalin, was a pivotal event in the region's history. While Durin's intentions were pure, the abyssal energy flowing through him made him a dangerous threat. His defeat, though necessary, was a sorrowful moment, leaving behind a legacy of both destruction and a strange kind of peace in Dragonspine.

Durin's remains in Dragonspine became a source of both corruption and unique alchemical materials. The Festering Desire sword, for instance, was forged from his remains and imbued with his corruptive power, which Albedo later used to study abyssal resistance.

Mini Durin, much like his historical counterpart, initially struggled with his dragon form, causing unintentional destruction in Simulanka. However, through the Traveler's intervention and the blessings of others, he underwent a remarkable transformation into a small, harmless plushy form. This transformation was a symbolic step towards overcoming the inherent destructive nature of his dragon form.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Durin's Human Form and Future Prophecies">
The narrative surrounding Durin's dragon form culminates in his miraculous transformation into a human. This monumental feat was orchestrated by Albedo, utilizing advanced alchemy and the goodness of Mini Durin to counteract the lingering abyssal influence of the original Dragonspine Durin. This event, revealed in Archon Quest "The Gardener" in Interlude Chapter: Act IV - "Paralogism" (Version 5.6), signifies a new chapter for the character.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Absorbing Suspo's Intelligence">
    The original Durin's core in Dragonspine absorbed the intelligence of Suspo (Albedo's failed homunculus prototype), granting the monsters under its influence shapeshifting abilities and inheriting Suspo's hatred for Albedo. This led to an attack on Mondstadt.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Tripartite Conference">
    Following the attack, Albedo called for a tripartite conference, involving the Hexenzirkel, the Anemo Archon (Venti), and the Knights of Favonius, to address the threat and plan Durin's ultimate fate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gathering Alchemical Reagents">
    Albedo required specific, rare alchemical reagents: the flesh of an immortal monster from the Mare Jivari and a portion of Rhinedottir's "embryo," the raw material used to create monsters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Draining Durin's Life Force">
    Albedo went to the original Durin's core in Dragonspine and drained its remaining life force, effectively laying the ancient dragon to rest and preparing it for fusion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Miracle of Human Transformation">
    During the Windbloom Festival, Albedo performed his miracle, fusing Mini Durin's inherent goodness with the remnants of the original Durin to create a human body.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The prophecy of Dragonspine Durin's eventual return was a long-standing mystery, but Albedo's intervention provided a unique resolution. The human form allows Durin to integrate into society, fulfilling his original desire for companionship without the burden of his destructive dragon form. He is now seen in Nod-Krai with Albedo, Alice, and his bestie, embarking on new adventures.

<Callout type="info" title="Future Implications">
While Durin has achieved a human form, the deep lore surrounding his abyssal origins suggests that his story in Teyvat is far from over. His presence in Nod-Krai hints at further narrative developments and interactions with other key characters.
</Callout>

</Section>
