<Section icon={Swords} title="The Genesis of Durin: Mini Durin vs. Teyvat Durin">
The character we know as the playable Durin in Genshin Impact is not a simple entity but a complex fusion of two distinct beings: the Mini Durin from Simulanka and the original Durin of Teyvat. This intricate origin story is central to understanding his identity and his relationship with his mother, Anya.

The original Durin, created by the alchemist Rhinedottir (also known as "Gold"), was a massive dragon whose remains are found in Dragonspine. This Durin was a product of Khemia, an art of creation distinct from Anya's narrative-based magic. The Mini Durin, on the other hand, was Anya's creation, born within the storybook world of Simulanka.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Durin's Maternal Affiliation: Anya over Rhinedottir">
A key aspect of Durin's character arc is his clear distinction between Anya Andersdotter and Rhinedottir as his mother figures. Despite sharing a physical connection to Rhinedottir's creation (the original Teyvat Durin's flesh), the playable Durin considers Anya his true mother due to her direct creation of his consciousness and soul.

This sentiment is reinforced through in-game dialogue and story quests. Anya's magical echo continues to guide Durin, solidifying her maternal role. The relationship between Albedo and Rhinedottir, where Albedo refers to her as "master" rather than "mother," further illustrates that creation through Khemia does not inherently imply a parental bond in the same way Anya's narrative creation does.
