<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Core Mechanics: Purity vs. Darkness">

Durin introduces a unique dual-state mechanic that dictates his role in a team. Unlike traditional Pyro units, his Elemental Skill, **Binary Form: Convergence and Division**, allows him to choose his state immediately upon casting. This flexibility makes him an invaluable asset for 2026 meta compositions, ranging from Overload to Forward Vaporize.

**Video Highlights:**
- **State Selection**: Using Skill + Skill enters **White Mode**; Skill + Normal Attack enters **Dark Mode**.
- **Energy Economy**: Durin restores a flat **30-33 Energy** upon entering a state, effectively lowering his Burst cost to 37-40.
- **Hexerei Buffs**: Having two Hexerei units on the team upgrades his RES shred from 20% to **35%** in White Mode.
- **Off-field Presence**: His Burst lasts **20 seconds** with an 18-second cooldown, ensuring 100% uptime.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapon Tier List: 2026 Rankings">

Durin benefits immensely from swords that provide Crit Rate or Attack percentage. Because his Burst does not snapshot, weapons with consistent or off-field triggers are preferred over those that require on-field build-up.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Signature: Athame Artis">
    The undisputed best-in-slot. It provides 33.1% Crit Rate and increases Burst Crit DMG. Its most powerful feature is the team-wide ATK buff that scales when the Hexerei resonance is active.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Premium Alternatives">
    **Primordial Jade Cutter** offers high Crit Rate and HP-to-ATK conversion. **Freedom-Sworn** is excellent for supportive playstyles, buffing the team's Normal/Charged attack damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Free-to-Play Options">
    **Finale of the Deep** (Fontaine Craftable) provides massive ATK but requires a healer. **Moonweaver's Dawn** is a free quest reward that offers solid Burst DMG and high base stats.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Wolf-Fang Limitation">
The **Wolf-Fang** (Battle Pass) is a strong stat stick, but note that its passive stacks **cannot be gained while Durin is off-field**. It is significantly better for an on-field Main DPS playstyle.
</Callout>
