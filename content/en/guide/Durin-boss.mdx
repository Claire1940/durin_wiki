<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Attack Patterns">

Facing the **Durin boss** requires a deep understanding of its mechanical phases. Unlike standard field bosses, the Mechanized Fortress utilizes a "Pyro Bar" system. While this bar is filling, the boss maintains significantly higher resistance to all forms of damage. Experienced players recommend focusing on survival and minor chip damage until the boss enters its overloaded state, where its defensive plating retracts.

The boss employs several high-damage maneuvers that can easily one-shot under-leveled or unprotected characters. Its melee repertoire includes a wide-range tail wipe and a devastating central saw blade rotation. At range, it fires cryo-infused projectiles that create lingering ice patches on the arena floor.

The ice patches left by the boss's ranged attacks do not dissipate immediately. Standing in these zones while the boss performs its spinning attack is the most common cause of team wipes.

<Section icon={Target} title="Ascension and Talent Materials">

To fully raise the character Durin, players must farm the **Durin boss** multiple times. A total of 46 **Cyclic Military Kuuvahki Cores** are required to reach Level 90. Additionally, players will need to hunt Fatui Oprichniki across Nod-Krai to collect various Warrants (Tattered, Immaculate, and Frost-Etched).

The local specialty required, the **Frostlamp Flower**, is found primarily between the Final Night Cemetery and the Frostmoon Enclave. These flowers are essential for every ascension milestone and should be gathered regularly as they respawn every 48 hours.

<Section icon={Steps} title="Step-by-Step Battle Strategy">

Successfully defeating the **Durin boss** involves a rhythmic cycle of evasion and burst damage. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth encounter.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement">
    Approach the Mechanized Fortress and trigger its activation. Immediately move to its flank to avoid the opening dash or tail wipe. Do not use your bursts yet, as its resistance is currently at its peak.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Resistance Phase">
    Focus on generating energy and maintaining shields. Watch the Pyro bar above the boss. Avoid the ice patches and keep the boss centered in the arena to prevent it from resetting.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Managing the Overload">
    When the Pyro bar is nearly full, the boss will begin a charging animation. Use Pyro attacks to speed up this process or prepare to dodge the ensuing explosion. Once the bar is full, the boss's defenses drop significantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Burst Window">
    Unleash all Elemental Bursts and skills. This is the only window where the boss takes 100% damage. Aim for the central core to maximize crit potential and potentially stagger the machine.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Reset and Repeat">
    After the vulnerability period ends, the boss will perform a massive stomp and reset its Pyro bar. Return to Step 2 and maintain your health until the next opening.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">
The boss arena is large, but the boss's attacks cover a significant portion of it. Always keep at least 25% of your stamina bar reserved for emergency dashes to escape the saw blade spin.
</Callout>

</Section>
